PlayStation Meeting宣布！美国东部时间9月7日下午三点

ratrosaw 2016-08-10 08:28编辑     64评论
IGN: Sony has confirmed, in an invitation provided to IGN, that the company will hold a PlayStation-focused event in New York this September.

The invitation does not elaborate on what will be specifically discussed at the event, which will be held in New York at the PlayStation Theater on September 7 at 3 p.m. ET. The invitation reads:

"We hope to see you there, and we can’t wait to share details with you about the PlayStation business."


IGN的报道：索尼已经向IGN发出邀请函，确认索尼公司将于九月七日东部时间下午三点在纽约PlayStation剧院举办一场有关PlayStation品牌的活动。邀请函并未说明活动的具体内容。邀请函的具体内容如下：“我们希望能看到您的加入，并且迫不及待的想要与您分享有关PlayStation事业的具体发展细节。”


（图片引自IGN报道）

Earlier today, reports from Vice Gaming and Gameblog claimed PlayStation would hold an event that day to unveil its PlayStation 4 Neo, also known as the PlayStation 4.5.

早些时候从Vice Gaming和Gameblog传出的消息，PlayStation将会于9月7日举办活动，首次对外展示PlayStation 4 Neo，亦称作PlayStation 4.5。）

Vice's report also states that final development kits will be sent to developers for the Neo around the time of the conference.


Vice的报告中也提到，最终版本的开发机将会在此次会议前后送达至开发者手上。

此次会议将会进行现场直播。
