原文来自Vgchartz

Capcom director and producer Jun Takeuchi teased a new PlayStation 4 game, in an interview with Famitsu and translated by Gematsu. He stated the game will be announced soon and that it "will even turn heads overseas."

"PlayStation 4 development has been ongoing, but the more I use the system the more it seems there is to uncover,” said Takeuchi. "SCE has made a really incredible system for us."

"Of course. Right now, we’re seeing how far we can push the game engine. You’ll be surprised by the quality of the games we’re working on right now. I think we’ll be able to announce it soon, so please look forward to it. I realize that’s obnoxious. It’s gonna be something that will even turn heads overseas."

One of Capcom's major new PS4 games is the free-to-play dungeon-crawler Deep Down. That game's producer, veteran Capcom designer Yoshinori Ono, is also working on a second PS4 title, according to Gematsu. It is possible that the game teased by Takeuchi is that game, though it's not confirmed.
________________________________________________________________________

卡普空金牌制作人竹内润日前在接受fami通的采访时透露了新游戏的开发进度。
他说：“我们一直在进行ps4的开发，随着开发进度，我们总能对ps4有新的认识。SCE的系统真的太棒了，索大好。”

“我们现在正努力看能把游戏引擎优化进化到何种地步。现在我们开发的游戏质量会让你们吓尿一秋裤。我们不久就会公布它了，敬请期待。我知道等待让人厌恶，不过，它一定会让全世界大吃一斤的。

免费的地下城探险游戏Deep Down也是卡普空主要开发的ps4游戏。哪款游戏的制作人小野也参加了另一款ps4游戏的开发。这很有可能就是竹内说的那款，不过这还没有确定。
