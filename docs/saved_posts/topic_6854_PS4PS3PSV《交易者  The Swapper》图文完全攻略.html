Message from Home
来自Home的消息

The Swapper's achievements are quite interesting : they give rise to an hidden side-quest, independent from the main story. The only hint about them is the list on Steam menu : I, II, III ...
In this way, they constitue the exploration part of the game, which complements the puzzle part.
The Swapper的奖杯很有意思：他们是从隐藏的支线任务而来，和主线无关。仅有的提示就是奖杯的描述：I, II, III ...
这游戏以这样的方式展现了游戏的探索部分，作为对解谜部分的补足。

There's 10 achievements : each one is a secret message to read on a console, located in an hidden room. Those « Message from Home » tells a bit more about the story.
一共有10个奖杯：每个都是在一个隐藏房间的控制台上读到的一条秘密消息。这些《来自家里的消息》对故事进行了补充说明。

Finding those hidden rooms by oneself is more rewarding ... but here's a guide if you're stuck with.
To refer to a precise one, an index is on the right →
自己找出这些隐藏房间会更有成就感...不过如果你卡住了，可以看下这篇攻略。

Basics [spoilerless hints]
基本说明（没有剧透的提示）

If you look for a little help but not a complete achievement walkthrough, read this section only. Further, there's a guide, and therefore spoilers ..!
如果你只想得到一点帮助而不是想看整个奖杯攻略，只读这一部分。另外，有攻略就有剧透……

The way to find them is more intuitive than it seems. Nothing is set completely randomly. The game leaves clues when an hidden room is near. Trust your good old video games's exploration reflexes ^^
找到隐藏房间的方法比看起来要简单。没有完全随机的内容，当附近有隐藏房间的时候，就能在游戏中找到一些线索。相信你多年玩游戏的直觉吧^^

Achievements's basics and hints :
奖杯说明和提示：

Achievements are unlocked once you read the corresponding message on the console.
在控制台上读完消息就会跳杯。

If you read one, then die or quit the game before a checkpoint, the steam achievement will be unlocked anyway, but not the Message from Home in the memory log of the game.
如果你读完一条消息，然后跪了或者在checkpoint之前退出游戏，那么奖杯虽然会跳，但是在游戏中的memory log里不会解锁。

Secret rooms always are hidden behind "solid-like" walls. They are reachable by swapping a clone through. When it's the case, your swapper's red ray is visible through those fake walls, instead of being stopped. (3 exceptions : , and require "physical" moves)
隐藏房间全都隐藏在“结实”的墙后。你可以交换一个克隆来到达。当这种情况出现的时候，你的swapper的红色光线会穿过这些假墙，而不是被阻挡了（有3个例外，需要“物理”移动）。

They all are allocated chronologically throughout the game (so if you find the II before the I, you'll find the missing one by retracing your steps) ... and quite equally (so as you progress, you can roughly evaluate where an hidden room is supposed to be).
他们全都是按照时间顺序分布在游戏中（所以如果你在I之前找到了II，那么你可以回溯你的足迹去寻找I）……而且大致平均分布（所以你在游戏过程中可以大致察觉到什么地方会有隐藏房间）。

If you meet useless paths, rooms or objects, they might be here for something ...
如果你发现了无用的路，房间，或者物品，那么他们很可能有某些隐藏用途……

None of them can be permanently missed. At anytime you can cross the map and reach them. But if you want to collect them all during the 1st play : Note that, if you end the game without having found all achievements, it's a good reason to give it a 2nd play !
这些房间不会永久遗漏。在任何时段你都可以穿过地图找打他们。但是如果你想一周目全部集齐：注意，如果你完成游戏之后没有找齐，那么这是开二周目的很好的理由！

奖杯攻略：隐藏控制台位置

Message from Home # 1
Area : Quarantine
Description : Watchers, red lights and asteroïds
来自家里的消息 # 1
区域：Quarantine
描述：看守者，红色的光，asteroïds



Set under the white spotlight and swap upward
走到白色的灯光下，然后换到上面


Then swap to the right to reach the hidden room
之后换到右边来达到隐藏房间

Message from Home # 2
Area : Gardens
Description : Spotlights, pit and plants
来自家里的消息 # 2

Swap to the pit's bottom left-hand corner
换到深坑下面左手边的角落


Then walk or swap to the left, through the plants ...
然后往左走或者换到左边，穿过植物……

→ Ⅲ
Message from Home # 3
Area : Cargo Bay
Description : Long sparkling travel-funnel
来自家里的消息 # 3
区域：Cargo Bay
描述：很长的闪光的通道


Start with all the clones (there's a clone-fizzler near to the right, behind a block)
造出所有的克隆后开始（右边有个clone-fizzler，在一个block后面）


Swap as far as possible inside the travel-funnel (be quick : you take it the wrong way !)
往通道里换克隆，越远越好（要快：你理解错了！#……尼玛，这是什么英文，我表示不能理解#）



Repeat instantly the move a 2nd time to reach the corridor's corner. Once above it, swap far downward
立刻重复上面的动作来到达走廊的角落。当你在那上面的时候，往下面换


... then at the bottom, swap the last clone through the wall to the right
之后在底部，把最后一个克隆换到墙的右边

→ Ⅳ
Message from Home # 4
Area : Laboratory Access
The place : Honeybee alveolus and broken electric thingy
来自家里的消息 # 4
区域：Laboratory Access
地点：蜂巢，破损的电弧（thingy）



Go down to the pit
跳坑


and swap to the left, behind the electric arcs (it hurts!)
换到左边，在电弧后面（疼！）

→ Ⅴ
Message from Home # 5
Area : Xeno Technology Research
Description : Teleport, moving antenna and floating boxes
来自家里的消息 # 5
区域：Xeno Technology Research
描述：传送，移动的天线，漂浮的箱子


Two little boxes ...
两个小箱子……


Climb on the upper box
爬到上面的箱子上


The hidden room is here, behind the upper left corner (lighted on the screen capture)
隐藏房间就在这里，在左上的角落（截图上点亮了）


Take a deep breath and jump high and far ! (swap unavailable)
深呼吸，然后又高又远地跳吧！（交换不可用）

Message from Home # 6
Area : Xeno Technology Research
Description : Puzzle
来自家里的消息 # 6
区域：Xeno Technology Research

Solve the puzzle, then visit the place this way :
解决谜题，然后这样：




→ Ⅶ
Message from Home # 7
Area : Sector 3 Lift
Description : Blue fog, Watchers and ceiling's gravity-switch
来自家里的消息 # 7
区域：Sector 3 Lift
描述：蓝色的雾，看守者，天花板的重力切换


(Another one without swapping)
（另一个不能交换的）


First, start from the previous room to the right, to reverse your gravity by walking on the gravity-switch. Then enter the room
首先，在前一个房间的右侧开始，走到重力切换处反转重力，然后进入房间


From the ceiling, run and jump over the gravity-switch (start jumping close to it!)
从天花板上，跑动并跳过重力转换处（靠近的时候再跳！）


... and continue running endlessly to the left
……之后一直往左跑

Message from Home # 8
Area : Solar Panel Maintenance Modules
Description : Big notice and chimney
来自家里的消息 # 8
区域：Solar Panel Maintenance Modules
描述：明显的提示，烟囱



Swap to the ceiling, a bit to the right
换到天花板上，稍微往右


Continue swapping up, along the right side
继续往上换，稍向右



Once you're high enough in the dark, the room is located on the right
当高度足够时，房间就在右侧

→ Ⅸ
Message from Home # 9
Area : Control Access
Description : Space forest and momentum !
来自家里的消息 # 9
区域：Control Access
描述：宇宙森林，动量




Start from the bottom of the room, and walk on the gravity-switch, to fall upward
从房间底部开始，走到重力转换处，之后往上落


Once in the column, wait till you're at the climax of your inverted fall
当你在这个纵列的时候，等一会直到你到了最高处


Then swap as far as possible to the top, several times ...
之后尽可能远得往上换几次……


... to reach the fake roof, and finally the room, by swapping to the left
……到达假屋顶，最后往左换到房间处

→ Ⅹ
Message from Home # 10
Area : Command
Description : Blue fog, Watchers and friendly elevator
来自家里的消息 # 10
区域：Command
描述：蓝色的雾，看守者，友好的升降梯


At the end of this blue room. No swap allowed. Just a high jump at the top of the elevator arrival :
在这个蓝色房间的最后。不能交换，需要在升降梯快到最高处的时候高高跳起


You'll have to increase your jump using the elevator's ascend force. Switch it on from downstairs ...
你需要利用升降梯的上升来增加跳跃的高度。在楼下开启升降梯……


... wait till the elevator is about to arrive, then at the last moment use its dynamic to jump to the upper right corner
……等到升降梯快到最高处的时候，跳到右上的角落


... Here. Well, continue to the right !
……这里，继续往右！

完成！
