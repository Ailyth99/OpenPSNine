What if I own Don't Starve: Console Edition (but no Reign of Giants) on PS4, how does cross-buy work for PS Vita?
If you own Don't Starve: Console Edition but do not have Reign of Giants on PS4 you'll be entitled to receive Don't Starve: Giant Edition for free on the PS Vita.

What if I own Don't Starve: Console Edition plus the Reign of Giants expansion on PS4, how does cross-buy work?
If you own both Don't Starve: Console Edition and the Reign of Giants expansion on PS4, you'll be able to receive Don't Starve: Giant Edition for free on PS Vita.

What if I own Don't Starve: Giant Edition on PS Vita, how does cross-buy work on PS4?
If you purchase Don't Starve: Giant Edition on PlayStation Vita, you'll be entitled to receive Don't Starve: Console Edition for free (but without Reign of Giants expansion) on PS4.


总的来说就是：
如果你有PS4本体，不管有没有“巨人国度”DLC，你都能免费获得PSV版。
如果你有PSV版，那PS4能免费本体，DLC需要另外买。

另外，本游戏没有Cross-Save。
