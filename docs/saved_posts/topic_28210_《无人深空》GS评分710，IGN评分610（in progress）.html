GAMESPOT 7/10
http://www.gamespot.com/reviews/no-mans-sky-review/1900-6416492/
+巨大，无缝的游戏世界
+优秀的技术为玩家提供了真情实感的旅程
+可以不受限制的探索各式各样的目标
+飞船和武器的设计十分吸引人
+有意义的叙事
-道具管理系统和系统交互界面设计较差
-多样性不足
-平庸的射击系统
-NPC缺乏个性，没有灵魂


IGN 6/10(IN PROGRESS)
http://m.ign.com/articles/2016/08/13/no-mans-sky-review
After more than 25 hours played I’ve yet to reach the goal of the center of the galaxy. Until I do this review will remain “in progress,” but from what I’ve heard of what lies ahead it’s unlikely to change significantly.

I still haven't quite reached the center of the galaxy, but if I had to score No Man's Sky today, I'd give it a 6.0, for Okay.
