PS4《看门狗 | Watch Dogs》首个媒体测评出炉

mechille 2014-05-22 22:56编辑     3评论


Watch Dogs is a great game that has a lot to do in the singleplayer but the multiplayer is painfully repetitive and not fun. The game stands on its own as a great singleplayer free roam but one that is outshined by Grand Theft Auto V.

看门狗在单人游戏有着丰富的游戏内容提供游玩
但多人游戏的部分容易重复且相当无趣
此游戏建立在单人游玩的杰出表现当之无愧
但在整体的游玩内容上没有超越GTA5
以及画面表现上确实不如E3所展示的那样

里面还有提到让个人非常在意的一个点：

One huge caveat/punishment Ubisoft implemented is when you do turn off the Online Option, all your skills accumulated are reset to zero. You read that right. They all get reset to zero

若关掉线上游玩的选项所有技能点数将会重置？！
