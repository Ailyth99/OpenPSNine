鼹鼠大冒险

  • Assassin's Deed

    Kill 10 bats during a single play of world 1 level 24.
    在世界1等级24中的一天内杀死10只蝙蝠
    3.80% 极为珍贵

  • Super Combo Combo

    Get over 125000 points on world 2 level 27.
    在世界2等级27中获得超过125000分
    3.90% 极为珍贵

  • Shining Light

    Make it through world 2 level 39 without ever turning off your headlamp.
    一次都不关闭前照灯的情况下通过世界2等级39
    3.70% 极为珍贵

  • Pure Pacifist

    Finish world 3 level 3 without killing any bats.
    不杀死任意一只蝙蝠的情况下完成世界3等级3
    4.80% 极为珍贵

  • Diamond Collector

    Collect 25 diamonds in world 4 level 1.
    在世界4等级1中收集25颗钻石
    5.60% 非常珍贵

  • Diamond Hunter

    Find 45 diamonds in world 4 level 40!
    在世界4等级40中找到45颗钻石
    3.60% 极为珍贵

  • Glutton for Punishment

    Collect all 19 diamonds on world 5 level 38.
    在世界5等级38收集全部19颗钻石
    3.50% 极为珍贵

  • Hold Your Breath

    Collect over 70 diamonds on world 6 level 19.
    在世界6等级19中收集超过70颗钻石
    3.60% 极为珍贵

  • Drowned Out

    Oh dear! You are no fish, you drowned 10 times!
    哦天哪！你不是条鱼，你已经溺亡10次了！
    10.30% 非常珍贵

  • Master Thief

    You showed the skill to get 3 stars on all levels in world 1.
    你在世界1的所有等级中都展示此技能以获得3星
    4.10% 极为珍贵

  • Super Stealthy

    You snuck past the obstacles fast enough to get 3 stars on all levels of world 2.
    你足够快的偷偷经过障碍物以在世界2的所有等级中获得3星
    4.00% 极为珍贵

  • Carried Away

    Using your new abilties, you were able to achieve 3 stars on all levels in world 3.
    通过运用你的新能力，你在世界3的所有等级里都能够获得3星
    3.70% 极为珍贵

  • Rock My World

    You blew away the rocks in record time and got 3 stars on every level of world 4.
    你在记录世界吹走了所有岩石并且在世界4的所有等级都获得了3星
    3.60% 极为珍贵

  • Super Sleuth

    These puzzles were no match for your sleuthing skills as you got 3 stars on all levels in world 5.
    这些谜题和你的侦探技巧如此不配，因为你在世界5的所有等级中获得了3星
    3.60% 极为珍贵

  • Water Mole

    You should be in the Olympics. You just swam your way through to 3 stars on every level of world 6.
    你应该参加奥运会。你在世界6的所有等级中都达到了3星
    3.60% 极为珍贵
