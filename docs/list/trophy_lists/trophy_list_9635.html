荒野大镖客

  • Red Dead Eye

    Complete a chapter with 100% accuracy.
    以100%的命中率完成一个章节
    4.90% 极为珍贵

  • Quick On The Draw 1 Tips

    Win a duel in Story mode.
    在故事模式中赢得一场决斗
    70.20% 一般

  • Full Boat 3 Tips

    Get a Full House in Showdown with Hold 'Em power ups.
    2.90% 极为珍贵

  • Hats Off 1 Tips

    Get six fatal headshots in six consecutive shots with a pistol in Story mode.
    在故事模式中使用手枪得到连续六次致命爆头
    4.80% 极为珍贵

  • Gunslinger 1 Tips

    Get a combo for over $500 in Story mode on any difficulty.
    在故事模式的任何难度下获得超过$500的连击奖励
    22.30% 珍贵

  • Untouchable 1 Tips

    Complete any chapter on Very Hard without taking damage.
    在超难难度下不受损伤完成任意章节
    1.60% 极为珍贵

  • Historical Account 5 Tips

    Complete the Journal.
    完成日记
    1.10% 极为珍贵

  • Well Provisioned 3 Tips

    Unlock all additional content available for purchase in the Shop.
    解锁商店中所有可购买的内容
    1.90% 极为珍贵

  • Out For Blood 3 Tips

    Complete every chapter in Bounty Hunter mode.
    完成赏金猎人模式下的每一个章节
    1.50% 极为珍贵

  • Peashooter

    Complete the game on Normal difficulty.
    以正常难度完成游戏
    15.10% 珍贵

  • Six Shooter 1 Tips

    Complete the game on Hard difficulty.
    以困难难度完成游戏
    2.20% 极为珍贵

  • Sharp Shooter

    Complete the game on Very Hard difficulty.
    以超难难度完成游戏
    1.30% 极为珍贵

  • Manny Quinn 1 Tips

    Complete a chapter in Red Wood Revolver mode.
    完成一章红木左轮模式
    1.80% 极为珍贵
