Strike Suit Zero

  • Transformer 1 Tips

    Transform into the Strike Suit for the first time.
    首次变身强袭装甲
    55.80% 一般

  • Strike Suit Evolved

    Unlock an upgrade.
    解锁一项升级
    39.40% 珍贵

  • Strike Suit Combo

    Destroy 5 targets at once in Strike Mode.
    强袭模式下一次摧毁5个目标
    20.00% 珍贵

  • Lucky Strike

    Destroy a Black Fleet interceptor with rockets.
    用火箭弹摧毁黑舰拦截者
    1.00% 极为珍贵

  • Strike Three

    Kill 3 or more targets with a single blast of the Raptor's cannon.
    使用猛禽加农炮一发干掉3个以上目标
    4.70% 极为珍贵

  • Shoot The Moon

    You can shoot the moon, we’re just not telling you how.
    你可以击中月亮，我们不仅仅是告诉你怎么做
    2.60% 极为珍贵

  • Strike Fast

    Complete the last mission in less than 7 minutes.
    7分钟以内通过最后一个任务
    1.00% 极为珍贵

  • Save Earth

    Reach the end of the game.
    Normal Ending达成
    6.80% 非常珍贵

  • Save Earth's Future

    Get a better ending.
    Good Ending达成
    1.30% 极为珍贵

  • Completist

    Unlock everything.
    解锁全部内容
    1.50% 极为珍贵

  • Obey Control

    No Win Scenario. Die by ramming into a torpedo or hull of a ship.
    死局，被鱼雷击沉或撞击舰体外壳
    1.50% 极为珍贵

  • Exit Simulation

    Beat the Heroes Of The Fleet campaign.
    舰队英雄任务战役通关
    2.10% 极为珍贵

  • Take One For The Team

    Survive a direct torpedo hit in the Marauder.
    掠夺者装甲模式下被鱼雷击中而不死
    1.40% 极为珍贵
