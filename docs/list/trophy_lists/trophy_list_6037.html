评分7

事实上除了短到令人发指的主线流程，我对本作几乎挑不出什么毛病来。
虽不是为次世代量身打造，但原爆点的画面也足够对得起ps4。操作相比以往也更人性化，无论是系列老玩家还是初次接触合金的新人都可以很快上手。各种细节上的调整都令人满意，让游戏的整体节奏流畅无比。整个游戏仅有的这一张地图也设计得足够精致，囊获了各种地形，玩家在达成目标时路线选择非常之多。另外本作也加入了一些前作没有的新要素，诸如载具的使用，可以呼叫直升机支援等。在通往主线后，还会解锁不少额外任务，其中不乏对系列的一些致敬，还有些恶搞元素。
可以说原爆点是一款继承了系列传统，但又不乏创新的合金装备。剧情方面也为之后的幻痛做足了铺垫。
最后说一下本作的主线流程长度，至多一小时可以通关，系列玩家半小时。一些游戏价值50hk左右的剧情dlc的流程长度都是本作的3，4倍之多。当然你可以挑战原爆点所有任务的最高难度最高评价，以及最速打法，都可以让游戏时间成倍增加。但仍然掩饰不了本作内容的单薄。再好的东西也应有它的价值，本作显然不值200多hk。

短短一部序章，耐玩度都很高

找了个老外对于原爆点继承要素的总结：

1. S Rank all missions（S完成所有任务）
There are a lot of youtube videos available of S-ranking all missions; I found it more fun to find my own way, but feel free to look them up if you're having trouble. In general, speed isn't as important as zero combat alerts + zero retries; the latter two are weighed heavily, whereas time provides less score. Essentially, the three most important things for most missions are to kill the fewest amount of enemies possible, zero combat detections, and zero retries.

2. Costumes（服装）
Unlocking character skins in Ground Zeroes will also unlock them for use in The Phantom Pain. (Source)

Costume List
Infiltration Suit – Own a copy of Metal Gear Solid V: Ground Zeroes.
Solid Snake – Recreate all scenes in side op “Déjà-Vu” NORMAL difficulty and complete the quiz after mission completion.
Gray Fox – Recreate all scenes in side op “Déjà-Vu” HARD difficulty and complete the quiz after mission completion.
Raiden (White Armor) – Complete side op “Jamais Vu” on NORMAL difficulty.
Raiden (Commando Armor) – Complete side op “Jamais Vu” on HARD difficulty.

You can find the quiz answers here.

3. Music（音乐）
I'm uncertain if music will carry over from your Ground Zeroes save to The Phantom Pain; obviously, it won't hurt to unlock all of the music anyways.

4. VIPs, HVTs, and Prisoners（基地人员）
In MGSV: TPP, certain people you rescue in Ground Zeroes will be available for use at Mother Base. (Source) All of these individuals only need to be rescued once in order for them to appear in TPP; what I'm unsure about is whether rescuing them on Hard difficuly or Normal difficulty matters, but I would rescue them on Hard just in case. In “Eliminate the Renegate Threat” and “Classified Intel Acquisition”, the targets are hostile, so you need to knock them out first.

Extraction List

Mission: Ground Zeroes – 7 Total
1. Chico
2. The four other POWs in Camp Omega, next to Chico
3. POW about to be executed (After rescuing Chico, interrogate guards to find his location in the refugee camp)
4. Paz

Mission: Eliminate the Renegade Threat – 4 Total
1. Glaz “The Eye”
2. Palitz “The Finger”
3. Two POWs in Camp Omega, same location as Chico

Mission: Intel Operative Rescue - 1 Total
1. Hideo Kojima (Complete the Mission)

Mission: Classified Intel Acquisition - 2 Total
1. Intel Agent (Signal him, then knock him out and extract him)
2. Bald Soldier (Ride the truck to the front gate, then head to the room beside where Paz was being held. Interrogate first.)

Mission: Destroy the Anti-Air Emplacements - 4 Total
1. POW in the Boiler Room, same location as Paz
2. Three POWs in Camp Omega, same location as Chico


Conclusion
That's about it. One thing that is entirely optional that I would recommend is catching up on the storyline of the series. Reading, watching videos, and playing through the old games are all viable options. Let me know if you have any questions or if I forgot something.
