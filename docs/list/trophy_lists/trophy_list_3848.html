    Walkthrough 2 - Ayamine Kei (彩峰慧)

    Now, from the main menu, choose "Premiere" then "Chapter". Press the right trigger to go to the next page and then choose まりもの死 under the Episode 7 column. Autoskip until the following two dialogue choices and choose the following;

    彩峰と……
    まずは彩峰だ……

    You can choose anything you like for the remainder of conversations in the game, just keep playing (autoskipping if you wish) until the game finishes. Be sure to save after the game ends.
