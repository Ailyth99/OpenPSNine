Tennis in the Face

  • Serves Them Right 1 Tips

    In a level with at least three enemies, knock everyone out with the first serve.
    在一个至少有3个敌人的关卡，一发之内击倒每个人。
    80.90% 一般

  • Sparing Bounces 1 Tips

    Knock out 5 enemies before the fourth bounce.
    在第四次撞击之前击倒5个敌人。
    29.80% 珍贵

  • Ball Saver 1 Tips

    Earn at least one extra ball from every level in the first district.
    在第一个区域每关至少剩下一个没有使用的网球。
    23.80% 珍贵

  • 25k 1 Tips

    Score 25,000 points with a single serve.
    一次发球赢得25000分。
    40.00% 珍贵

  • Tennis In The Face 1 Tips

    Get five headshots with a single serve.
    一次发球打出5次爆头。
    39.10% 珍贵

  • 100 Bounces 2 Tips

    Serve a ball that bounces 100 times.
    发出一个能造成100次撞击反弹的球。
    17.40% 珍贵

  • Explodz Junkie 2 Tips

    Knock out six opponents with a single Explodz explosion.
    用单独的一个爆破罐干掉6个敌人。
    5.90% 非常珍贵

  • Glass Destroyer 1 Tips

    Destroy 10 pieces of glass during one level.
    在一个关卡内摧毁10块玻璃。
    30.70% 珍贵

  • Main Menu Enthusiast 1 Tips

    Earn 1,000,000 points in the main menu.
    在主菜单赢得100万分。
    6.80% 非常珍贵

  • Vending Champion 1 Tips

    Destroy three vending machines with a ball and a single serve.
    在一次发球中用同一个球毁掉3个自动售货机。
    13.50% 非常珍贵

  • 10tons Defeater 1 Tips

    Defeat the 10tons crew.
    击败10tons的开发者们。
    5.20% 非常珍贵

  • Cash Cow 1 Tips

    Collect three briefcases with a single serve.
    在一次发球中收集3个公文包。
    19.50% 珍贵
