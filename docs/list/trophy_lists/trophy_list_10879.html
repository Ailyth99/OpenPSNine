枪火游侠

  • Champion of Champions 4 Tips

    Unlock all trophies in Paladins.
    获得《Paladins》（枪火游侠）中的所有奖杯
    0.20% 极为珍贵

  • Champion of the Realm 7 Tips

    Get 4 Champions at Mastery 5.
    4位英雄达到精通5（最初免费的部分英雄不纳入人数计算）
    13.10% 非常珍贵

  • Master Collector 12 Tips

    Unlock 60 Talents.
    解锁100张卡牌（通过解锁英雄等级获得）
    16.50% 珍贵

  • Untouched 5 Tips

    Win a match where the enemy team scored no points.
    在敌方没有获得分数的情况下赢得一场占领护送
    74.30% 一般

  • Questions Later 9 Tips

    Deal damage to every enemy champion in a match before any of them damaged you.
    在敌方英雄对你造成伤害之前，抢先对他们每个人造成了伤害
    23.00% 珍贵

  • Boom Headshot 7 Tips

    Land 5 headshots in a row without missing.
    没有松开扳机的情况下一次性连续爆头5次
    46.90% 珍贵

  • Last One Standing 20 Tips

    Be the only player alive in a match.
    成为比赛中唯一幸存的玩家
    1.20% 极为珍贵

  • Send Off 3 Tips

    Kill a player with an environmental hazard.
    利用地形击败一位敌方玩家
    42.80% 珍贵

  • Not Like This 3 Tips

    Kill an enemy player while you are at or below 50 Health.
    在生命值低于50时击败一位敌方玩家
    67.20% 一般

  • Double Kill

    Get 10 Double Kills.
    10次二连击败
    39.60% 珍贵

  • Survivor 6 Tips

    Survived more than 50 battles with less than 10% health.
    生命值低于10%存活超过50场战斗
    7.30% 非常珍贵

  • Hard Carry 5 Tips

    Kill more than 20 enemy champions in a single Siege match.
    在一场占领护送比赛中击败超过20名敌人（助攻不算）
    30.10% 珍贵

  • Give 110 Percent 1 Tips

    Get a killing blow after the Round has ended.
    在回合结束后击败一个敌人
    36.60% 珍贵

  • Bounty Hunter 13 Tips

    Kill an enemy player who is on a 15 killstreak or higher.
    终结一位连杀数至少为15的玩家
    7.40% 非常珍贵

  • I Like Big Numbers 3 Tips

    Deal more than 100,000 damage during a match.
    在单局比赛中至少造成100000点伤害
    34.30% 珍贵

  • From the Grave 3 Tips

    Kill an enemy champion after they had already killed you.
    在你已经被击败的情况下击败一名敌人
    33.00% 珍贵

  • Gone Spelunking 1 Tips

    Die to an environmental hazard.
    死于环境伤害
    47.80% 珍贵

  • Sniper 4 Tips

    Kill an enemy player from over 300 units away.
    击杀一位300码以外的敌方玩家
    10.30% 非常珍贵

  • Counter-Air Defense 2 Tips

    Kill a player in the air who is over 100 units above the ground.
    击败空中距离地面100码以上的玩家
    73.20% 一般

  • Teamed Up 8 Tips

    Played a match while in a party.
    与好友列表中的一位好友组队完成一场比赛
    45.10% 珍贵

  • Well Trained 3 Tips

    Complete the Paladins Tutorial.
    完成新手引导
    51.60% 一般
