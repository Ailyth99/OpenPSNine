    下面就解释一下系统，至于选项请参考原帖。

    种地：
    前两三天基本上是拔草，等到可以去Flowershop，就买草莓和黑莓种子来种，别的种子不考虑。
    种地操作的优先级建议这样考虑：1、除虫 2、播种 3、收获 4、除草
    卖菜卖到900多就可以停止了，可以按三角键Skip Working。（卖到1000以上有一个杯，但是攻略女主的时候要求1000以下）

    一周六天的安排选项有Running、Flowershop、Library、Stay home和Relax。
    Running：增加Determination
    Flowershop：增加Romance
    Library：增加Cluture
    Stary home：增加Coolness
    Relax：增加Health。每周安排一天Relax，Health过低会有惩罚。

    攻略思路就是4项平均发展，都过70后存档（一般是3 weeks Left的时候差不多了）。
    然后S/L单独发展某一项，就能达成对应的那个女主的结局。
    还有一个Alone结局，就是读档一路睡过去就是了。

    女主攻略过程的选项如下，各个女主出现的事件是随机的，不过一般都会出全。
    毕竟你有10多周去找她们，事件一共就4、5次，不是每次找她们都会触发的。

    Jill (Stay Home)
    Choice 2: Ask her what happened at work.
    Choice 1: Make a joke.
    Choice 1: Ask how he's doing.
    Choice 2: Encourage her to go for it.
    Choice 1: Congratulate her.

    Clara (Running)
    Choice 1: Say you like sports.
    Choice 2: Don't say anything.
    Choice 2: Tell her to wish her father well.
    Choice 1: Agree to keep going.

    Susana (Flowershop)
    Choice 1: Agree to help.
    Choice 3: Say you're afraid of bees.
    Choice 2: Tell her that you're willing to wait.
    Choice 3: Susana.
    Choice 1: Leave.
    Choice 1: Say you understand.
    Choice 2: Offer to make some tea.

    Mariana (Library)
    Choice 1: Offer to hold the ladder for her.
    Choice 1: Thank her for everything she's done.
    Choice 2: Offer to get some food her her.
    Choice 2: Say that there's nothing weird about liking older women.
    Choice 2: Tell her to snap out of it.
    Choice 1: Encourage her to do it.

    Unckle Sam
    Choice 1: Say you feel guilty for being rude to him now.
    Choice 1: Say it's really difficult.
