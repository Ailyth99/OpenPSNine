奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • langrisser2
    Ensure you have the following game settings before starting:
    Damage= Low
    Round Count= Best 2 out of 3
    These ensure you have enough time to get your special meter built and do not defeat the opponent first.
    With Player 1, pickStorm.
    With Player 2, pickSentinel.
    Select any map.
    Build up the Special Meter withStorm.
    HaveSentinelcontinuously jump.
    UseHailstormwhileSentinelis in the air.
    If it does get you at least 50 hits then repeat the steps as needed.
