Out For Blood

Complete every chapter in Bounty Hunter mode.

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • kashima_naoki
    先通关STORY MODE 解开赏金猎人模式
    然后赏金猎人模式完成以下关卡解锁对应金手指

    Bite The Bullet (Cheat)
    Complete the "Fort Diego" level bounty hunter mission

    Golden Gun (Cheat）
    Complete the "The Siege" level bounty hunter mission

    Infinite Dead-Eye (Cheat)
    Complete the "Battle Finale" level bounty hunter mission

    Invincibility (Cheat)
    Complete the "Fall From Grace" level bounty hunter mission

    No Distortion (Cheat)
    Complete the "End of the Line" level bounty hunter mission

    Sad Stars (Cheat)
    Complete the "The Mine" level bounty hunter mission

    No HUD (Cheat)
    Complete the "Stagecoach" level bounty hunter mission

    这个算是所有奖杯里最需要技巧和耐心的奖杯了，赏金模式和故事模式的区别是有许多额外目标，比如之前提到的金钱连击，爆头，无伤和寻找一些东西，如果觉的有困难可以先打解锁金手指的关卡，再开金手指做，难度会降低不少

  • gavin-fw
    Infinite Dead-Eye (Cheat) 无限死亡眼，Invincibility (Cheat) 无敌，Bite The Bullet (Cheat)伤害加倍受伤加倍，Golden Gun (Cheat）金枪。只有这四个有用优先做，赏金猎人模式做完这四个其他的关也可以开金手指，有要打COMBO 的关把金枪和伤害加倍关了。一般开无限死亡眼➕金枪➕无敌就够了。其余的基本就是一些画面效果，没太大用处。
