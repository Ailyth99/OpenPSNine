The Conqueror

Complete all bonus objectives in the Empire of the Rising Sun campaign

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • hjuy65tghmmmx3
    在战役中会以NEW BONUS OBJECTIVE(新的额外目标) 作指示，在雷达地图上以黄色的惊叹号作标示。帝国战役总共有 14 个额外目标，完成这些额外目标不一定会增加战况优势。

    第 1 章：Vorkuta - The Death of Father Frost (沃尔库塔—急冻之父之死)
    Destroy all Barracks (摧毁所有的军营)
    Capture the Hospital (占领医院)

    第 2 章：Stalingrad - To Conquer Shattered Spirits (史达林格勒—征服虚弱的灵魂)
    Destroy All Soviet Monuments (摧毁苏联所有的纪念碑)

    第 3 章：Odessa - Behold the Mighty Saint of Swords (傲德萨—看这强大剑圣)
    Destroy the Landmarks (摧毁地标)

    第 4 章：Pearl Harbor - Graveyard of a Foolish Fleet (珍珠港—愚蠢舰队之墓)
    Capture the Long Range Radar Towers (占领远距离雷达塔)
    Defend the Long Range Radars (保护远距离雷达塔)

    第 5 章：Pacific Ocean - Assault on the Black Tortoise (太平洋—突袭玄武)
    Escort the Engineers to the Power Cores in 3 Minutes (在3分内保护工程师抵达电力核心)

    第 6 章：Santa Monica - Rage of the Black Tortoise (圣塔摩尼加—玄武之怒)
    Capture The International Airport (占领国际机场)
    Destroy the Observatory Cannon (摧毁瞭望台炮塔)
    Destroy the Amusement (摧毁娱乐场)

    第 7 章：Yokohama - Barbarians at the Bay (横滨—港湾蛮人)
    Capture the Nanoswarm Hives (占领奈米虫群)
    Destroy the Soviet Forces (摧毁苏联军队)

    第 8 章：Moscow - Crumble, Kremlin, Crumble (莫斯科—粉碎，克里姆林宫，粉碎)
    Capture the Soviet V.I.P. Bunkers (占领苏联V.I.P.的地堡)

    第 9 章：Amsterdam - The Last Red Blossom Trembled (阿姆斯特丹—颤抖的最后红花)
    Neutralize the Proton Colliders (摧毁质子撞击炮)
