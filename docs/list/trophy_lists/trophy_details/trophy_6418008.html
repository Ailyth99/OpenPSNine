Shiv Doors

The Outskirts
1.Downtown - Look for the white door to your right, just after you've been attacked by the first Clicker.
2.Museum - Once you've become separated from Tess and Ellie, enter the cafe that's to the left of the room with the horse statue. The door you need is on the right as you enter.

Bill's Town
3.The Woods - When you enter town after the woods, look for the building to your left after the second drop.
4.The Woods - After making your way through the town, you'll encounter a Clicker that detonates a trap of Bill's. Go around the corner from here and the Shiv Door will be on your left or right, depending on which way you approached it.

Pittsburgh
5.Alone and Forsaken - Enter Kingston Bookstore, then go to the second floor up. You'll find the next Shiv Door inside Rivers Cafe, to the right.
6.Alone and Forsaken - After the cut-scene, when Joel's hiding behind the taxi, look for the taxi door with a red X marked on it.
7.Financial District - Head to the back-room of the Credit Union with the Irrational Depot sign over it. The Shiv Door you're looking for is blue.
8.Escape the City - After you've met up with Sam and Henry, head down the corridor and into the stairway area. After going one flight down, look for the wooden Shiv Door right in front of you.

Tommy's Dam
9.Hydroelectric Dam - There's a point in this part of the story where you need to turn a valve. The door is to the right of this valve.

The University
10.Science Building - After you've clambered into the building via the truck, head down the corridor, then past the doors and the vending machine until you reach the end. The Shiv Door is in the furthest right-hand corner.

Lakeside Resort
11.Cabin Resort - Head over the road from the petrol garage until you come to a hotel room with a missing door. Climb through the broken bathroom window and the door you're looking for is to your left.

Bus Depot
12.Underground Tunnel - After Ellie opens the door ahead of you both, open the Shiv Door on your right before heading into the tunnel on the left.

The Firefly Lab
13.The Hospital - The final Shiv Door is located on the sixth floor of the building. Go around the right-hand corner to find it.

一共13个门，正确做法是，在一次通关过程里+通关后的选章节补=在主页统计里明确看到开门记录是13或者12即可，这里说12可能有歧义，别急。这个统计数据似乎只适用于一个周目里，如果你点了新游戏，统计数据就清0了，因此不能说下周目我只开上周目没开的门就行了，不行。
如果在一次从头到尾的通关里把13个门全开了，当然能跳杯。
但是如果没开够，系统统计你的开门就是你在这个周目从头到尾通关过程里开过的门，补的时候注意，比如说我知道我哪个门没开，我统计数据是10，我选章节跑图进去开了，退出这个章节前看了一眼显示数据是11，已经涨了1，这个时候不能退，必须打完这个章节，回到主页里的统计数据才能是+1的，你退了就不统计。除非你是12，就差一个，那你跑过去当场开了统计变13，那无所谓了直接退，因为杯已经跳了退出去统计还是12也没关系。
