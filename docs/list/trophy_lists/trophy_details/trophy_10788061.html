    Bear Station: 10 Melee Kills
    This challenge can be found in the area between Spawn and the Mess Hall. You can get this very easily by getting the challenge before scene 10 and using a melee weapon from the Spawn room to kill 10 zombies.



    Whitetail Deer Docks Station: Rebuild 45 window boards
    This challenge can be found in front of the Bait & Tackle shop at the docks near the fire barrel. An easy way to do this is to grab it in the later scenes past scene 10 and getting the Quickies perk to rebuild barriers faster. Spawn area has an abundance of barriers to rebuild so go there in order to find the most barriers to build.



    Thunderbird Station: Kill 25 Crawlers
    This challenge can be found between the Bait & Tackle shop and the Spawn area. An easy way to do this is to grab it in the later scenes past scene 15 and using the Ben Franklin crossbow or Frag/Gas Grenades to create crawlers to kill.



    Wolf Camp Station: Get 10 Multikills
    This challenge can be found near the entrance of the Wolf Camp. This can be done easily by either using the Acid Rain crossbow or a powerful Pack-a-Punched Sniper Rifle and getting collateral kills.



    Owl Camp Station: 25 long-distance kills
    This challenge can be found within the Owl Camp between 2 cabins. This challenge can be easily completed by using an LMG, Assault Rifle, or Sniper Rifle. Creating a train and running far away from them to be far away enough for the kills to count for the challenge.
