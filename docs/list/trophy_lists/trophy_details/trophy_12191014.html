    10250硬币
    38 Kukuli Stones
    29 Cheranka Trees
    1 Capla Tapla种子
    5 Gamumuht斧头
    1 GiGi蜜糖
    2 Bocchollo羽毛
    2 Nella Tree Saps
    2 Dokkola树根
