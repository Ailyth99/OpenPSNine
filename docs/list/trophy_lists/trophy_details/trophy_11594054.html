    1 Starting place 起始点– The place where you start the exploration of the dungeon.

    2 Red key 红钥匙– The location of the red key. Aquiring the key is optional - you don't need it in order to advance.

    3 Red gate 红门– The location of the red gate - you need a red key to open it. Opening the gate is not mandatory - you can reach the area behind it through a corridor.

    4 Mini-boss 中BOSS鳄鱼- Crocodilian – A room in which you will be fighting a mini-boss, Crocodilian.

    5 Blue key 蓝钥匙– The location of the blue key.

    6 Blue gate, yellow key, mini-boss Crocodilian 蓝门，打死中BOSS鳄鱼拿到黄钥匙– The location with the blue gate that can be opened with a blue key. A mini-boss encounter with the Crocodilian awaits you here. Once you defeat him, you can collect a yellow key.

    7 Yellow gate 黄门– The location of the yellow gate - you need a yellow key to open it.

    8 Green key 绿钥匙– The location of the green key.

    9 Green gate 绿门– The location of the green gate - you need a green key to open it.

    10 Boss - Baron – A room in which the boss of the dungeon, the Baron, awaits you.

    11 Second red key 第二把红钥匙– The location of the second red key. Acquiring it is optional - it's required to open a gate leading to a treasure. It's not required to complete the mission.

    12 Second red gate 第二道红门– The location of the second red gate - you need a red key to open it. There's a room behind the gate with a hidden treasure.
