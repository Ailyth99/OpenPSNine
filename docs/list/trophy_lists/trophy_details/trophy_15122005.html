    This can be done in the hub area near the end of the game. In the cryogenics area of the hub, you will find a cleaning bot that is turned off. Activate it and carry it. There will be another cleaning bot moving around the hub. Carry one bot to the other and let go of the bot near the other one. If you have completed the game, you can reload your game and backtrack from the end to any part of the Tacoma.
