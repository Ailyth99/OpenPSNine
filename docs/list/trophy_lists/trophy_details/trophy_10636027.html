    Wild West Grill House F101
    Bacon (check the tables and in the kitchen)
    BBQ Chicken (on some tables)
    BBQ Ribs (check the tables)
    Beer (all over the dining area)
    Steak (in the back kitchen)
    Vodka (behind the minibar)

    Lobster (on the stove)
    Pasta (on some tables)
    Pineapple (next to the soda fountain)
    Wine (grouped on the middle counter)

    Cheesecake Mania F103
    Brownie (on the back counter)
    Cake (behind the display glass)
    Cookies (on the back counter)
    Donut (behind the display counter)
    Ice Cream (in front of the checkout counter)
    Milk (near the back counter)
    Pie (behind the display glass)
    Whiskey (near the Maintenence Room)

    Lombardi's F104
    Jellybeans (in the plastic containers)
