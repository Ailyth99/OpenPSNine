Friends With Benefits

Had relations higher than 90 with all factions in the Modern Era

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • l-sh4wnc-l
    Sandbox 模式，摩登时代 ，200 人口，无限金钱，经济和政治难度都 very low，无灾害，三种胜利条件都不要开。

    （注意：以下建造全部用 quick build）
    暂停游戏，造一个 Power Plant 一个 Nuclear Power Plant，随便选几条公路延伸建造方便之后造建筑。
    接着建造：
    32 个 Office；
    3 个 Cathedral
    4 个 Military Fort
    7 个 Cigar Factory
    30 个 Apartment
    1 个 Space Program
    1 个 Nuclear Program
    80 个以上 Large Garden

    然后快进，等着收奖杯就行了。

    如果发现派系关系里有哪一项不足 90 的话可以加建对应的建筑，参见附录：

    Militarists Like- Police Station, Guard Tower, Military Fort, Barracks, Army Base, Aircraft Carrier, Nuclear Program

    Religious Like- Catholic Mission, Church, Cathedral

    Communists Like- Country House, House, Mansion, Tenement, Apartment, Modern Apartment, Clinic, Hospital

    Capitalists Like- Oil Refinery, Oil Well, Lumber Mill, Textile Mill, Cannery, Steel Mill, Pharmaceutical Company, Fashion Company, Cigar Factory, Rum Distillery, Jewelry Workshop, Vehicle Factory, Chocolate Factory, Electronics Factory, Mansion, Supermarket, Bank, Customs Office, Office, Airport, Aerodome

    Environmentalists Like- National Park, Gardens, Waste Treatment Plant, Hang Gliding Club, Cabin, Country Hotel

    Industrialists Like- Geothermal Plant, Oil Refinery, Oil Well, Lumber Mill, Textile Mill, Cannery, Steel Mill, Furniture Factory, Pharmaceutical Company, Fashion Company, Cigar Factory, Rum Distillery, Jewelry Workshop, Vehicle Factory, Chocolate Factory, Electronics Factory,

    Nationalists Like- Nuclear Plant, Nuclear Program, Space Program

    Globalists Like- Airport, Aerodome, Lighthouse, Embassy, Office
