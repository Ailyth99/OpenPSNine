奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • langrisser2
    Miscellaneous Specific:
    DifficultyandRound Countdo not matter.
    Easiest (0 Stars)
    Round Count 1 (First to 1)
    Get the first hit on every single round.
    This means not just at the start of the fight - it must be after each time you knock the opponent down.
    Make heavy use ofSave Statesat the start of each round to ensure you can get the first hit (then make a Save State after the first hit).
    Play onMANUAL/NORMALsetting andnot AUTO.
    This disables the auto-blocking system.
    This is selected after picking your Character.
    Connect with aHyper Xmove at least half of the rounds.
    Again, using Save States can help ensure your Hyper X moves land correctly.
    Must win every round.

    Character Specific:
    Colossus- 3-Hit combo required.
    Wolverine- 8-Hit combo required.
    Psylocke,Omega Red,Sentinel- 10-Hit combo required.
    Cyclops- 17-Hit combo required.
    Spiral- 21-Hit combo required.
    Storm- 25-Hit combo required.
    Silver Samurai28-Hit combo required.
    Iceman- 50-Hit combo required.
