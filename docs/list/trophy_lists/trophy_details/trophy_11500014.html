    我玩的是英文版，一共17个词头：
    Brilliant
    Charming
    Dulcet
    Epic
    Fleeting
    Grim
    Intrepid
    Lofty
    Malleable
    Noble
    Prickly
    Rampant
    Savage
    Troubling
    Vibrant
    Wild
    Wondrous

    其中10个词头有3个种类：Figment, Fancy, Fantasy
    另外7个只有Fantasy，有：
    -Malleable Fantasy
    Reward for Sora's fourth, fifth, and sixth special portals in Traverse Town, fifth and sixth on Le Cite des Cloches, and fifth and sixth on the Grid,
    -Prickly Fantasy
    Reward for Sora's fourth, fifth, and sixth Secret Portals inside Prankster's Paradise.
    -Wild Fantasy
    Reward for all three Special Portals as Sora inside The World that never Was. The first 2 portals have a chance to award it, while the third has a guaranteed drop.
    -Epic Fantasy
    Reward for Riku's fourth, fifth, and sixth Secret Portals inside Prankster's Paradise and fourth, fifth, and sixth Secret Portals inside Country of Musketeers.
    -Charming Fantasy
    Reward for Riku's fourth, fifth, and sixth Secret Portals inside Traverse Town, Le Cite des Cloches, and the Grid.
    -Brilliant Fantasy
    Reward for Riku's sixth Secret Portal in Symphony of Sorcery and third on The World that never Was, possible on first and second in the same world.
    -Savage Fantasy
    Chest inside Avenue of Dreams in World that never Was, possible reward for all Secret Portals in the same world for both characters. Rare drop from Tyranto Rex and Ryu Dragon.
