Top Collector

Wesley, the owner of the shop Panda Bear, is an avid trading card collector. Show him your own collection. 64 cards will get you a "super rare thank you." His shop hours are from 20:00 to 06:00.

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

可触发章节：7-9, 11-16, 18, 26.
触发地点：熊猫熊商店[Panda Bear]，与韦斯利对话[Wesley]。
任务流程：收集到64张卡片后去找韦斯利对话。
任务所得：无限寿命的光剑[Light Sword (Infinite)]。
