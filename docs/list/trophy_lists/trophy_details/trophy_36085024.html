    复活技能的合成公式

    You can get Revivify by performing the following Skill-Binding chain:
    Combine Heal and HP+20 to get Heal II
    Combine Heal II and Cyclone to get Healing Wind
    Combine Healing Wind and HP Recovery to get Healing Wind II
    Combine Healing Wind II and Cure to get Revivify

    Alternatively, you could use this Skill-Binding chain:
    Combine HP + 50 and Mighty Roar to get Healing Wind II
    Combine Healing Wind II and Cure to get Revivify
