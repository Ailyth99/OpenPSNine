    对照一下，去对应的章节拿就好

    Troll Funeral Rites: Chapter 2 - Do not interrupt Snow's speech. Go to the table on the left and view the cards.
    Headless Horseman: Chapter 4 - Crane's apartment on top of his desk. View the statue. It's best to visit here first, just not last. If you go their second, make sure you tell Bluebeard you're heading their first.
    Flycatcher: Chapter 4 - At Dee and Dum's Office, you'll talk to Flycatcher. It's best to visit this place second, just not last.
    Glamour Tube: Chapter 5 - Spare Aunty Greenleaf's tree.
    Glamour Tree: Chapter 5 - Burn Aunty Greenleaf's tree.
