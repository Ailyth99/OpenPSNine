    Found the answer to this elsewhere, this was unfortunately some really shoddy game design as far as achievements are concerned, as I thought I had all the plants and spent hours playing with nothing else to unlock, only to realise that the plants grow based on both time , but also saving checkpoints and going to the ship, here is the process:

    Once you have all 10 plants / your last plant, travel to Bogano, run to the first checkpoint and save, then quit to main menu.

    Continue your game, head to the ship, you last plant will now have grown to stage 1 (half grown), run back to the checkpoint, save there and quite to main menu.

    Continue again, run back to ship and check out your terrarium for achievement.
