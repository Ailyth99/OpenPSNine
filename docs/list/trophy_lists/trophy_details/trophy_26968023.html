风之主 (Master of the Wind)

请召唤旋风。

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • shizhengzhi
    1. Go to the far left side of Arkos Underpass, above Combbelton Cemetery.
    *You'll know it's the right spot when you walk over a green platform you can jump down.*
    2. Go left past the green platform to where there's a small stone ledge next to the edge of the level.
    3. Stand in the far left corner against the wall, and crouch.
    4. After 5 seconds or so, a tornado will appears, picking you up, and setting you down.
