Blaze Fingers

Rapid fire your good old gun

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • return_cn
    相当玄学的一个杯，v版我是通关过程中自然解开的，4版却花了很长时间来触发

    日站的说法是：“銃を敵が多い場所で何発も撃ち続けたら取得出来ました。”
    欧美的说法是：“You can get this trophy, when you get your gun in the tutorial. What you have to do is push the trigger, which is Square or R2, very very fast. So set your controller up, in any fashion you want, and just mash either of the two buttons as fast as you can, until the trophy pops. I tried mashing both at once, but that didn’t seem to work for me. So I set my controller down and used both my thumbs to alternate hitting Square.”
    以及：“If like me you don't fancy watching a 30 minute video to figure out how to get this achievement. Just spam the RT as fast as you can whist using the starting gun.”
    还有：“For anyone having issues with this such as i did.
    Start level one ,City, and press RT as fast as possible reload and try again.
    If the achievement does not unlock Then press LT where it looks like the gun goes behind your back and repeat. Smash RT AS FAST as possible reload and do it again. LT seems to switch your gun and is barely noticeable but ,after a hour of trying, hitting LT and trying worked”

    我个人光靠提高按的手速没有成功，最后是重新安装游戏后在第一关一遍往前走一遍狂按成功了（杀敌*2），所以到底哪些要素是必要的，真的成了一个玄学的迷……
