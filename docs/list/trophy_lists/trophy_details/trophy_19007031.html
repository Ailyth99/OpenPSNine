    The lore books are interactable objects that you can use to get a little more detail about the plot and the setting.
    用來加深對劇情和世界觀瞭解的物件叫lore book。游戲中有像“Orders from Ranger Command”這類文件，“Slaver's note”這類筆記/便條，“Survivalist's diary”這類日記，“Time capsule letter”這類信件。這些都統稱lore book，不僅僅是書本。
    至於爲什麽把lore book譯作｢逸文｣，見問答
