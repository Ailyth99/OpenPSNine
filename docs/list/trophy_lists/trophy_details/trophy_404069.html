Possible Glitch: At this point you may encounter a glitch I had (it's quite rare, but should still be mentioned). It could happen when the first "boss" spawns (it could also happen with all mentioned boss-enemies). All enemies could just climb up and jump down again - no one attacked attacks until the time runs out. As a result, you could basically wait around for about 4 minutes! It's not quite known what triggers this glitch, but if you notice that they just climb up and jump down again don't shoot anyone - just stand around and you normally don't have to fight anymore.
