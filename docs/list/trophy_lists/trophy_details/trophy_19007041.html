Badda Bing

You blew up 6 enemies with a single explosive attack.

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • omega_neko0
    Badda Bing=bada bing=bada bing bada boom, used to signify the end of a process or chain of events thought to be difficult but actually easy.

    最先想到的是粵語中的表達「話咁易」「易過借火」，但是覺得不夠書面，後來想到了「易如反掌」，但是又沒有上兩者生動。最後聯繫獎盃圖標和說明，決定譯成「簡單到爆」
