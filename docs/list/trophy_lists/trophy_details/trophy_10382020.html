    Select Single player => Open World => Crazy Granddad, the gist of this mode is drive around and try to find special "crazy granddad" objects. You only need one for this but the trophy won't pop until you run out of time so once you pick one up, either let your time run out or have a drive around but don't worry about finding them as you have a huge arrow that directs you straight towards them.
