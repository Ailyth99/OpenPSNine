  • hysgdtc
    Maximum Life - Unlocked from the start（最大生命数-初始存在）
    Extra Gem - Found at the top of the Tower of Patience (See Is this a TOWER!?)
    额外宝石-耐心之塔顶部
    Extra Time - Found after defeating the Blood Ghost, found in 1-2 (See I Ain't Afraid of No Ghost)
    额外时间-击败1-2的血幽灵后获得
    Range Up – Found after defeating World 1 boss (See No Goo can stop me !)
    范围提升-击败第一幕boss后获得
    Fire Rate - Found after defeating One Eye in 2-2 (this enemy will appear if you purchase a weapon in 2-1)
    射速-打败2-2的单眼怪后获得（2-1购买武器后出现单眼怪）
    Huge Gem - Found in treasure room (see Treasure Room)
    巨大宝石-财宝房内发现
    Sharpening Stone - Found after defeating the Shadow Reptile (see The Shadow)
    磨刀石-击败阴影蛇后获得
    Contract of Reaper - Found after defeating World 4 boss (see I want my gold back !)
    死神契约-击败第四幕boss后获得
    Twin Axes - Found after defeating World 3 boss (See You shall not drill !)
    双斧-击败第三幕boss后获得
    Shield - Found after defeating World 2 boss (See Temple Raider)
    盾牌-击败第二幕boss后获得
    Magic Glasses - Found on hidden platform in 2-4. To find the hidden platforms, go to the large empty space of the level and fire your weapon until it appears to hit something. Fire a few more times to get the hidden platform.
    魔法眼镜-2-4的隐藏平台发现（特定场景概率出现）
    Power Up - Found in 4-2 or 4-3 on the hidden platforms. See above for how to reveal hidden platforms.
    力量加强-4-2或4-3的隐藏平台发现（特定场景概率出现）
