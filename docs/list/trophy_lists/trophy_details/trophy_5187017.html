零秒出手

在一季的 MyGM 所有 41 場主場比賽中，在比賽終了前投進致勝壓哨球。

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

这个奖杯翻译错了，sold out 明显是脱销的意思，图标上也是一张门票
正确来说，是指41场主场比赛，门票脱销
You can get this trophy by using the Miami Heat or the OKC Thunder (default rosters) or the Custom Lakers team used for the other MyGM trophies. With these teams you should be able to sell out all the home games, especially if the team is doing very well. You could also change the prices but will need to acquire level 1 Business Savvy which costs 500 VC. Just put low prices and make sure that you have a high % of fan interest to sell out games.
