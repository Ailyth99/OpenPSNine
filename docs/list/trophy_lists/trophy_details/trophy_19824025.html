在Samurai Shodown IV中施展「No Contest」

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • tvb_skawaks
    断末奥义：下 下 上 前 前 + C
    在游戏中挑衅对手一次（按三次开始键 - PS4默认是pad键），尽量每局在40秒钟之内干掉对手，此时在最终局中使用A键将对手击败（A键按住不放），那么就会百分之百出现断末奥义提示了。

  • langrisser2
    Basic conditions to get more chances of No Contest are:

    ·The enemy must be stand on the floor when the last slash attack ends with his Life Gauge, and such hit must make him lean forward (as opposed to knocking down or leaning back).
    ·Defeat the opponent in 30 or less seconds during the winning round.
    ·Use Weapon-Toss Provocation (push Start button 3 times) at least once during the match.
    ·Hold Light Slash button while use other buttons to finish the opponent.
    When the match finish and the conditions are met, the stage background turns in grayscale and a message written in kanjis appears on screen, alongside this command:

    down down up forward forward + C (C button equals Strong Slash in Neo Geo), which is the same for every character.
