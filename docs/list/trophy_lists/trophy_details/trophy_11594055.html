    1 Starting place 起始点– The place where you start the exploration of the dungeon.

    2 Blue key 蓝钥匙 – The location of the blue key. You don't have to acquire it - you can reach the further part of the dungeon without opening the blue gate.

    3 Mini-boss 中BOSS鳄鱼(可选) - Crocodilian – A room where you will encounter a mini-boss, Crocodilian. Fighting him is optional.

    4 Blue gate 蓝门– The location of the blue gate - you need a blue key to open it.

    5 Yellow key 黄钥匙– The location of the yellow key. The key is required to get to the futher part of the dungeon.

    6 Yellow gate 黄门– The location of the yellow gate - you need a yellow key to open it.

    7 Red key 红钥匙– The location of the red key. It's required to get to the boss of this dungeon.

    8 First red gate 红门(8、9任选一)– One of the two red gates leading to the boss of this dungeon. You can go through any of them - it doesn't change anything.

    9 Second red gate 红门(8、9任选一)– One of the two red gates leading to the boss of this dungeon. You can go through any of them - it doesn't change anything.

    10 Boss - Viscount – The room in which the boss of this dungeon, the Viscount, awaits you.
