Spring Cleaning

Complete all Investigations in the base within a single campaign

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • hackjak
    在每个主线任务之间，把基地的全部区域都走走，把闪光的东西都摸一下，有感叹号的NPC一定要去说话，会交给你一些小任务，完成会解锁派遣任务和次要任务。在第一章找美女通讯关对话选250.北，892，会解一个支线，务必完成
    以下是影响奖杯的调查任务清单：
    Radio Interference (After Mission 1: Invasion!)
    Find the Missing Silacoids (After Mission 2: The Doctor and one Minor Operation)
    Sleepwalkers (After Mission 3: Signal from Beyond)
    Keeping Things Secure (After Mission 3: Signal from Beyond)
    Vendetta (After Mission 3: Signal from Beyond)
    Test Flight (After Mission 4: Not of the Earth)
    The Cure (After Mission 4: Not of this Earth)

    特别注意调查2：Find the Missing Silacoids
    After "Mission 2: The Doctor" and whichever Minor Operation you chose.
    Go to the laboratory. Doctors (Heinrich) Dresner and Weir will be talking. After Weir gives you the tour of the lab, go upstairs to find Heinrich.

    In order to find the Silacoids, you need to use your Battle Focus ability (B Button) to track their glowing trails. However, they're always in the same place. The first will be in the airlock when you leave the lab; the second is in the offices downstairs, in the male toilets, and the last group of them are upstairs, near Weaver's office. Destroy them all and return to Heinrich.
