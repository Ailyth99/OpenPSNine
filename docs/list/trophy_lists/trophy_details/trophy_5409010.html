Math McGrath

Use multiplier and combiner blocks to get a chain of x10 or more! Will not unlock in puzzle mode.

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

用X3、X2和X？达成10倍以上倍率的消除。
例如：
x2 + x3 + x3 + x2 = x10
x3 + x? + x3 + x3 = x10
x? + x3 + x2 + x2 + x3 = x11
