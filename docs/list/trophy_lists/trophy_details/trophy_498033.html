The Closer

Complete all bonus objectives in the Allies campaign

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • hjuy65tghmmmx3
    在战役中会以NEW BONUS OBJECTIVE(新的额外目标) 作指示，在雷达地图上以黄色的惊叹号作标示。盟军战役总共有 19 个额外目标，完成这些额外目标不一定会增加战况优势。

    第 1 章：Brighton Beach - Ride of the Red Menace (布莱顿海滩—红色威胁来袭)
    Garrison Four Civilian Structures (驻守四座民间建筑物)
    Capture the Hospital (占领医院)
    Repair the Coastal Guns (修复沿岸的炮台)
    Train 5 Javelin Soldiers (训练五名标枪军人)
    Build 5 Multigunner IFVs (建造五辆多功能步兵战斗车)

    第 2 章：Cannes - The Shark and the Lure (坎城—鲨鱼及诱饵)
    Neutralize the Defenses Guarding the Vindicators (破坏那些防御维和轰炸机的防御系统)

    第 3 章：Heidelberg - The Famous Liberation (海德堡—驰名的解放运动)
    Assist Agent Tanya (协助探员谭雅)

    第 4 章：Gibraltar - Enemy of Our Enemy (直布罗陀—我们敌人的敌人)
    Eliminate the King Oni (消灭鬼王)
    Set Up an Expansion Base in the Southwest (在南西方扩建基地)

    第 5 章：North Sea - The Unfathomable Fortress (北海—深不可测的要塞)
    Destroy the Generators (摧毁发电机)
    Capture The Generators (占领发电机)

    第 6 章：MT. Rushmore - A Monument to Madness (拉什莫尔山—狂人纪念碑)
    Destroy the Power Plant (摧毁发电厂)
    Capture the Boot Camp (占领新兵训练营)
    Capture the Jefferson Head Control Center (占领杰斐逊的控制中心)
    Capture the Lincoln Head Control Center (占领林肯的控制中心)
    Capture the Washington Head Control Center (占领华盛顿的控制中心)

    第 7 章：Tokyo Harbor - Forever Sets the Sun (东京港—永不见日)
    Destroy The Imperial Blockade (摧毁帝国軍的包围)

    第 8 章：Havana - The Great Bear Trap (哈瓦纳—巨熊陷阱)
    Recruit a Force of 10 Soviets (招募十名俄军的单位)

    第 9 章：Leningrad - The Moon Shall Never Have Them (列宁格勒—月亮永远无法到手)
    Destroy the Vacuum Imploder (摧毁真空内爆装置)
