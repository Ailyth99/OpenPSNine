Pre-Hard Mode:



Amber Mosquito: Rarely obtained from the Extractinator 精炼器低几率掉落.
Beeswax: Note that this item was originally named Honeycomb but the name was changed in the 1.02 patch. Found in chests in the Underground Jungle and can also be dropped by Dragon Hornets (Underground Jungle)丛林宝箱或龙蜂掉落.
Bone Key: Random drop from the Dungeon Guardian which shows up when you try to enter the Dungeon without killing Skeletron first击败地牢守护者100%掉落(非骷髅王).
Brain: Randomly found in Gold Chests and Shadow Chests. Since the 1.02 patch it can also be found in Lihzahrd Chests (Jungle Temple) and is dropped by The Groom (only spawns during Blood Moon events)黄金、暗影、蜥蜴宝箱随机获得，血月的Groom僵尸也可以掉落.
Cabbage: Randomly found in Chests and Gold Chests. Since the 1.02 patch it can also be dropped by Dragon Snatchers (Jungle)宝箱与黄金宝箱随机获得，丛林的食人花小怪也掉落.
Eater's Bone Random drop from Eater of Worlds世界吞噬着boss掉落.
Fish: Randomly found inside Ice Chests (Underground Snow)雪地的寒冰宝箱掉落.
Nectar: Random drop from Queen Bee蜂后boss掉落.
Petri Dish: Randomly found in Gold and Ice Chests, and can also dropped by Shadow Slimes (Corruption biomes) during Hardmode黄金与寒冰宝箱掉落，肉山后暗影史莱姆也可掉落.
Seaweed: Rarely found inside Ivy Chests in Jungle Shrines (Underground Jungle)丛林中的藤蔓宝箱掉落.
Vial of Blood: Randomly found in Gold Chests and can also be dropped by Vampire Miners (Cavern layer)黄金宝箱与吸血鬼矿工掉落.
Tartar Sauce: Rarely obtained from Iron Crates (found by fishing)钓鱼获得的铁箱子中获得.
Toy Sled: Random drop from Ice Mimics (Snow biomes)冰宝箱怪掉落.
Wolf Fang: Randomly found in Gold Chests and can also be dropped by Werewolves (only spawn during Full Moon events in Hardmode)黄金宝箱与狼人掉落.
Zephyr Fish: Rare chance to catch while fishing. Increasing your fishing power will increase your chances of catching it. Using a Sonar Potion while fishing is highly recommended钓鱼时稀有掉落.

Hard Mode:



Baby Grinch Mischief's Whistle: Random drop from Ice Queens during the Frost Moon event霜月寒冰女王掉落.
Cursed Sapling: Random drop from Mourning Woods during the Pumpkin Moon event南瓜月悲伤木头掉落.
Eye Spring: Random drop from Eyezors that only spawn during a Solar Eclipse (Hardmode event)日食眼魔掉落.
Lizard Egg: Random drop from Flyings Snakes and Lihzarhds (Jungle Temple)丛林神庙中飞蛇与蜥蜴人掉落，0.03%掉率我真是醉了.
Parrot Cracker: Sold by the Pirate NPC when he is in an Ocean biome海边住所中的海盗出售.
Seedling: Random drop from Plantera世纪之花掉落.
Spider Egg: Random drop from Pumpkings during the Pumpkin Moon event南瓜月中南瓜王掉落.
Strange Glowing Mushroom: Sold by the Truffle NPC蘑菇人出售.
Tiki Totem: Sold by the Witch Doctor NPC when he is in a Jungle biome during Hardmode丛林住所中的巫医出售.
