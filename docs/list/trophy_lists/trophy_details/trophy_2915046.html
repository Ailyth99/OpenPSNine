      异常状态总共有：魅惑、中毒、恐惧、混乱、冰冻、感电、封魔/沉默、激昂、消极
      伊丽莎白：用L1或者圆圈+叉捕捉对方上Debuff，SP在0-49是魅惑（心心），50-99是中毒（血槽每秒掉两小格血），100-149是恐惧（骷髅），150是混乱（星星和电）。一共能解4种
      小熊：按圈键丢东西，能丢到感电（身上发黄光+雷电），冰冻（大冰块），激昂（身上发光），中毒（暗黑料理）。一共能解四种
      直斗：被对方打到觉醒后滑摇杆搓招式，“下 下前 前 下 下前 前”+方块 攻击对方，，一套连招的最后是放SP子弹，打中对方后会上封魔/沉默。一共能解一种。

    There are 8 ailment types:
    - Poison: your HP gradually decreases
    - Panic: your controls will be reversed
    - Rage: your strength is increased but you become unable to block
    - Charm: your opponent drains some of your SP
    - Mute: you cannot summon your Persona
    - Fear: every hit is considered to be a fatal counter
    - Shock: you will be unable to move
    - Freeze: you will be unable to do anything

    Now comes the list of characters who can inflict those ailments to you:
    Elizabeth can do almost all of them. Most comes from her Furious Action (A+B) based on Elizabeth's current SP:
    0-49 inflicts Charm
    50-99 inflicts Poison
    100-149 inflicts Fear
    150 inflicts Panic
    Her Mabufudyne can inflict Freeze (QCB + X or A)
    The boost version of her Maziodyne can inflict Shock (QCF + A+X)

    Teddie can put you into enraged state through his Furious Action (counter move) or through his regular B move (he throws random things on screen, you are looking for a drink)

    And for last, Naoto can inflict you with Mute, just get hit by her Anti-S SP Pistol alpha (QCF, QCF + X).

    The most easy way to net this achievement is playing online with a partner in player match.
