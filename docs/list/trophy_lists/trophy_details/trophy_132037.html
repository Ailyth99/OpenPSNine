No More Strangers

You've met all the random characters.

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

ximuhan2015-12-27 13:30
注意这个奖杯要求仅仅是“met”见过所有的陌生人，并不要求你一定要完成所有陌生人的任务，我在通关过程中杀掉了Dwayne的女朋友，以为做不了cherise的任务了，差点重开。其实杀掉的都算“见过了”，没有什么可遗漏的。另外Key To The City100%游戏的奖杯对此也没有什么要求， Jeff， Ivan， Clarence ， Cherise的任务都不计算在内。还有就是我在解此杯的时候经常到地点找不到人，有些人的出现有固定的时间段，并不是全天都在，附列表如下：


Dukes/Broker
Brian

Brian 1:
Time: I found him around noon/12:00
Task: Nothing, he will give you 100$

Brian 2
Time: You will need to wait two days again until he shows up again
Task: Take him to the dealers.
Drive him home

Brian 3:
Time: You will need to wait 1 in game week or a little longer. I found him at around 3am in the morning
Task: Drive him to the deal.
Kill both of the dealers who are attacking him.
Drive him back to his house.


Mel
Time: Try it around noon/12:00
Task: Drive Mel to the meeting place
Kill the 3 guys.
Drive her back.


Badman
Time: I found him around noon/12:00
Task: Drive to the red marker
Kill the Russian gang




Ilyena Faustin
Time: I found her around noon/12:00
Task: Meet her daughters boyfriend
Kill him or beat him up




Algonquin


Cherise
*Note: If you killed her in the mission “Ruff Rider” She will not appear here for obvious reasons
Time: I found her around 3pm/15:00
Task: Drive to the marker.
Beat up the boyfriend.
Return to Cherise.


Clarence
*Note: If you kill him in the mission “Blood Brothers” he won’t appear here for obvious reasons.
Time: Around noon/12:00
Task: Kill him.



Jeff

Jeff 1:
Time: I found him around 5pm/17:00.
Task: Follow his wife to a café
Go inside and take a photo of the couple

Jeff 2:
Time: He will call you 2 days after his 1st task
Task: Meet him.
Dump the body/car in the water.

Jeff 3:
Time: You will need to wait a week until he shows up again
Task: Nothing, watch the cut scene.


Pathos

Pathos 1:
Time: Any time
Task: Kill the guys who are attacking him

Pathos 2:
Time: I found him around noon/12:00
Task: Kill the attackers.
Drive him to the hospital.




Hossan:
Time: I found him around 3pm/15:00
Task: Collect the money.
Chase after the guy who attacks Hossan (kill him).
Drive him to the yellow marker.




Marnie

Marnie 1:
Time: Around noon/12:00
Task: Take Marnie to the dealer's place.

Marnie 2:
Time: Around moom/12:00
Task: Take Marnie to the station.



Sara

Sara 1:
Time: She only shows up at night
Task: Drive her home

Sara 2:
Time: She showed up at around 5pm/17:00 for me.
Task: Go to the store
Buy or kill the guy to get the item.
Deliver it to Sara.


Alderney


Eddie Low
*Note: He will only appear between 20:00 and 4:00. He showed up at 2am/2:00 for me
Eddie 1:
Time: 20:00-4:00
Task:Take him to the docks
Drive him home

Eddie 2:
Time: 20:00-4:00
Task: Kill Eddie (he will attack you with a knife)



Ivan
*Note: You can kill him in the mission “Actions Speak Louder than Words”. He won't appear here for obvious reasons.
Time: Around 3pm/15:00
Task: Collect his money (kill the guys)




Gracie:
Time: Around noon/12:00
Task: Kill the Guys who are attacking you.


Hope this helps you!!
