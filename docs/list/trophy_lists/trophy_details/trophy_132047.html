Top Of The Food Chain

You successfully killed 20 players with a pistol in a deathmatch.

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

在Party Mode里与好友开始一场Deathmatch，并设置如下：

Game Settings:

Location: Happiness Island
Duration: $2,500
Weapons: Pistols
Traffic: Parked
Pedestrians: Off
Blips: All
Online ID Display: On
Respawn Distance: Near
Respawn Time: 1 second
Time of Day: Midday
Weather: Clear

在比赛过程中刷够20人就会跳杯，然后换人解。
