    看英文攻略这个是可能错过的奖杯，贴上英文攻略：
    After you get to the Black Gorges, you will come across a monk named Bartur, who will give you the side quest "Seeds for Jabo". Follow the marker for this quest to find Jabo in the Jungle. Talk to him and ask about what problem he is having to be told that his watchdog is not behaving the way he wished. Find the watchdog (actually a Shadowbeast) near the greenhouse and defeat it, then return to Jabo to report your success and get this trophy.
