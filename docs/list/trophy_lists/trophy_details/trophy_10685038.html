全拯救流程可以触发otis全支钱电话，有些生还者没被拯救会导致无法触发后续支线，就会错过电话
第一天去全部的区域至少一次就会有对应区域的电话，太晚了就没有对应区域介绍，错过电话
每个主线case的第一部分触发时间到了之后、过期之前的一个小时内，不在屋顶或者安全屋，otis就会给你打电话，直接去触发主线就会错过电话。

订正一下，需要必须救的NPC一共有五个 ，分别是Floyd Sanders, Kindell Johnson, Cheryl Jones, Paul Carson,还有Simone Ravendark，另外除了这五个人之外每个房间要多有三个幸存者才能触发后续事件。
WIKI原文：Ronald, Kindell, and Floyd need three other survivors in their rooms for their scoops to activate. Possible survivors to save in Kindell's room include (any 3): Jeff, Natalie, Leah, Josh, Barbara, Rich, Gordon, Jennifer, and Wayne. Possible survivors to save in Ronald's room include (any 3): Bill, Burt, Aaron, Sophie, Heather, and Pamela. Possible survivors to save in Floyd's room include (any 3): Tonya, Ross, Sally, Nick, Jolie, and Rachel. Other survivors in these rooms do not appear until after you would get the scoop calls, so they do not qualify. So in total you must save fourteen survivors for the Transmissionary achievement as a bare minimum. Further details are on the Survivors and Scoops pages
