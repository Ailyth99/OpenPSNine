    分数是如何计算的？

    Destroying a plane or helicopter: 30 points.
    摧毁一架飞机或直升机得到30分。

    People wasted: 1 point each. Points will only be added to your total after your wanted level is cleared or the game is saved (i.e. if you get busted or wasted during a killing spree, points will not be tallied).
    每杀一个人得1分，分数只会在你的通缉等级清除后或保存游戏后加入你的总分（如果你在虐杀中被捕或死亡了，分数是不会被计算的）。

    Accuracy: 5 points per every 1%.
    每提升1%的命中率即可获得5分。

    Criminals killed on Vigilante - 1
    point each. These are in addition to any points received under category two, and you aren't required to clear your wanted level for the points to count.
    在协警任务中每杀死一个罪犯减少1分？？？（我怀疑这写错了，应该是获得1分吧）

    Cash held: 1 point per $5,000, up to a maximum of 200,000 points for maxing out your bank balance at $999,999,999.
    每携带5000$就会得到1分，达到金钱上限($999,999,999)后可获得200000分。

    Getting busted or wasted: -3 points.
    被捕或死亡一次会减少3分

    Cheating: -999 points. Only occurs with particular cheats (e.g. vehicle spawns.)
    使用某些特殊作弊码会减少999分(如生成车辆)
