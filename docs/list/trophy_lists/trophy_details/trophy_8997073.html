tarran_19892016-08-30 22:56
@tarran_1989问题已解决，在外站上查到原因了。原文如下:

1、​Expel Barstow from the vault during the quest "Better Living Underground". This is the fastest option to get this trophy but it will prevent the rest of the quest line for the DLC from occurring, and doing this may lock you out of obtaining the Bronze Vault Dweller trophy.

2、Complete all the quests, which takes 1-2 hours, and then talk to the overseer again at the end of "Lady Luck". At this point, she will want to leave the vault to share her new knowledge with the rest of the vaults, or she will leave due to being disatisfied with the results. However, with a speech challenge, she will stay, though you will still receive the same rewards. Completing the mission by keeping her in the vault will void the trophy.

3、Killing the overseer at any time will also cause you to become the new overseer. If you choose this method, it is recommended to wait until after you complete all the quests to do so.


大意就是，获得这个奖杯的途径：1、最快的办法就是在最开始的任务的时候就杀了女尸鬼，你就直接成为监管人了，但是会影响后续奖杯获得。2、在最后一步任务完成后，有个对话选项，女尸鬼提出要走，这时候如果你魅力够的话可以说服他留下，这样即使你完成任务也不会获得这个奖杯了，坑爹啊（我就是这样）。3、如果你上一步选择让他留下没有获得奖杯的话，可以直接选择把她杀了，这样就能代替他成为监管者了，这样做请务必确保你已经完成所有的任务。

总而言之，这个任务就是你和女尸鬼只能留一个，，，简直坑爹，我以为我卡杯了，吓一跳。。。。再说一句，，，TM的终端机必须切换英文，而且终端机也必须是在英文模式下新造的才行，B社XXXXXXX
