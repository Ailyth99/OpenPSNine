Use every type of firearm on a zombie

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • lincoln_bws
    对僵尸使用各种枪械，确保各种枪械击杀至少1只僵尸。枪械获取地点如下：
    Assault Rifle- Food Court - Hungry Joe's Pizzeria的雨棚上
    Handgun- Palisades Mall 2楼 - High-noon Shooting展示柜上
    Shotgun- Palisades Mall 2楼 - High-noon Shooting架子上
    Sniper Rifle- Palisades Mall 2楼 - High-noon Shooting架子上
    LMG- palisades mall中央有水的酒吧 - 南部右侧的岩石上
    Merc Assault Rifle- Underground地下 - Americana Casino地下的维修室（maintenance room）
    Blast Frequency Gun- Underground地下 - 上一把枪的位置往左走，在地图西北部的斜坡上
    Six-Shooter- South Plaza - On Buzz Saw By the Big Statue(enter the plaza and head to your left. either do the WWJWD? mission now or have a look at the buzz saw by the statue to get the six-shooter)
