Cold Pot 5

Sigourney often wanders the town when the weather is nice. Having been told that her "pot is getting cold!" you now have to take her home. What's the truth behind this pot?!

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

可触发章节：7-9, 11-16, 18, 26。
触发地点：西格妮[Sigourney]，天气晴朗时她会在哈利的别墅[Harry's Mansion]附近。
任务流程：尽快将她载回家，越快越好。载她回去之后就可以发现她手中锅子的秘密了。
任务所得：绿谷套装（[Greenvale] outfit）。
