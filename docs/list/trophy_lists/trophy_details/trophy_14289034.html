    After cleaning up a district, you will unlock a special Pixar family build in that area, which you'll be placed in front of it after clearing the district. Complete the build to unlock an Easter egg from a previous Pixar movie. You will also unlock a character from that movie as a playable character. That character will have the special ability that you need to unlock a red brick in that build. Once you do the last Pixar family build, you will have all 12 red bricks. The red bricks are very helpful in completing the game, so it is recommended you get them all after going through all the story levels once. https://youtu.be/CGS6qiGfjaU
