    This requires you to find the 7 housecats, located throughout the station as part of the AR data sequences.

    Cat Locations:
    1.) Personnel / Conference Room - 0:28
    2.) Personnel / Laundry - 1:06
    3.) Biomedical / Imaging - 1:27
    4.) Food Supply / Protein Growth - 1:49
    5.) Engineering / Power Cell Access - 2:12
    6.) Engineering / Networking - 2:47
    7.) Hub / Docking - 3:18

    If you have completed the game, you can reload your game and backtrack from the end to any part of the Tacoma.
