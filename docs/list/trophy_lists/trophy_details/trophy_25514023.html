  • lepingps
    How to unlock MUS A M20
    如何解锁MUS A M20
    MUS A M20 is the second boss you'll encounter through normal gameplay. Defeat him to earn this trophy!
    MUS A M20是你在正常游戏中遇到的第二个boss。击败他赢得奖杯！
    His location is Lake Amagi (25,18).
    他的位置是阿马吉湖（25，18）。
    The strategy for him is different than all the other bosses. He can't be hit by normal attacks, all you can do from the start is Disable his attacks by Hacking different parts of his body (which is definitely recommended so he can't hurt you at all!). Camp by a Dispenser and hack him until he can't be hacked any more.
    他的策略不同于其他所有老板。他不能被普通攻击击中，从一开始你所能做的就是通过攻击他身体的不同部位来禁用他的攻击（这是绝对推荐的，这样他就不会伤害你了！）。在一个分发者那里露营并攻击他，直到他不能再被攻击为止。
    You need to find the Shockwave Ability at (23,20), then use that on the stalactites as he swims by. Once you hit him with around 5 or so, he'll die, giving you this trophy!
    你需要在（23，20）处找到冲击波能力，然后在他游泳经过的钟乳石上使用它。一旦你用大约5点左右击中他，他就会死，给你这个奖杯！

    需要R3装备古德安纳，先去装置攒几个气泡，然后在BOSS经过的路上钟乳石附近R3释放能量，让石头打中BOSS，如此几次就能打掉BOSS。
