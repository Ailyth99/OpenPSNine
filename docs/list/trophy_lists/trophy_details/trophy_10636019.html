    写在最前：需要地下通道钥匙(完成Case 2-2)，按照下列获取物品以及制造顺序，快速完成所有武器制作
    1.">"代表组合
    2."--"代表收集或者其他

    Safehouse
    --获取Fireaxe
    --获取Queen(击杀带蜂后的僵尸，或者用橙汁+橙汁的混合果汁)

    Royal Flush 靠近安全屋的维修室
    >Drill Bucket (Drill + Bucket)
    >I.E.D (Propane Tank + Box of Nails)
    >Air Horn (Pylon + Spray Can) - Spray Can 在商店"In the Closet"
    >Spiked Bat (Baseball Bat + Box of Nails)
    >Defiler (Sledgehammer + Fireaxe) - Fireaxe安全屋里就有
    >Tenderizers (MMA Gloves + Box of Nails) - MMA Gloves在商店"The Man's Sport"
    --获取更多的钉子Nails (可能需要通过切换场景的方式多获得点钉子)

    Royal Flush 东侧的维修室 - 靠近地下通道入口
    >Driller (Drill + Spear)
    --2楼 "Children's Castle"外的树下获取2个Dynamite
    >Sticky Bomb (Lawn Dart + Dynamite) - Lawn Dart在跑车右侧的树下（靠地图边缘）
    >Blambow (Bow & Arrow + Dynamite) - Bow & Arrow 在"Chieftan's Hut"，以及刚才获取的Dynamite
    --在"Chieftan's Hut"获取Spear

    Underground Access 地下通道维修室 - 靠近Royal Flush
    >Spear Launcher (Leaf Blower + Spear) - 刚才在Royal Flush获取的Spear
    >Freezer Bomb (Fire Extinguisher + Dynamite)
    --获取 Dynamite
    --找到购物车，从另一条路走（左转），到下一个站台
    --获取BFG(Blast Frequency Gun) (靠近购物车) - 返回Royal Flush

    Royal Flush 在商店 "Tunemakers"里的维修室
    >Super B.F.G. (Blast Frequency Gun + Amplifier)
    >Power Guitar (Electric Guitar + Amplifier)
    --从现在起，如果合成材料离维修室很近的话，就不再复述。

    Americana Casino 维修室
    >Fountain Lizard (Lizard Mask + Fountain Firework)

    Fortune City Arena 维修室
    >Pole Weapon (Push Broom + Machete)

    South Plaza 维修室 - 靠近 Arena
    >Plate Launcher (Plates + Cement Saw)

    South Plaza 西南边的维修室
    >Paddlesaw (Paddle + Chainsaw)
    >Rocket Launcher (Rocket Fireworks + Lead Pipe)

    South Plaza 东南边的维修室
    >Auger (Drill Motor + Pitchfork)
    >Ripper (Cement Saw + Saw Blade) - Saw Blade 在维修室外北边
    --获取在此附近的Gasoline Canister

    South Plaza 靠近 Fortune City Hotel 的维修室
    >Beer Hat (Construction Hat + Beer) - 都能在一个圆形的区域里找到
    >Exsanguinator (Vacuum Cleaner + Saw Blade)

    Platinum Strip 靠近电影院的维修室
    >Parablower (Leaf Blower + Parasol)

    Platinum Strip 靠近Arena的维修室
    >Handy Chipper (Wheelchair + Lawnmower)

    Fortune Park 靠近 Royal Flush的维修室
    >Snowball Cannon (Fire Extinguisher + Watergun)
    >Flamethrower (Water Gun + Gasoline Canister) - Watergun在喷泉里，Gas Canister 是在South Plaza获取的
    --Royal flush入口前面，靠近棕榈树的草地上获取Football

    Fortune Park 存档点旁的维修室
    >Portamower (2X4 + Lawnmower)
    >Electric Rake (Battery + Leaf Rake)

    Fortune Park 靠近Atlantica的维修室
    >Hail Mary (Football + Grenade) - Grenades在购物车附近的草地上，Football刚才已获取
    >Dynameat (Hunk of Meat + Dynamite) -Dynamite from Underground
    >Knife Gloves (Bowie Knife + Boxing Gloves)

    Silver Strip 靠近Slot Ranch的维修室
    >Roaring Thunder (Battery + Goblin Mask)
    --从"One Little Duck Bingo"获取Bingo Ball Cage
    --从维修室旁的梯子上楼来切换场景，重新刷新并获得Battery
    >Tesla Ball (Bingo Ball Cage + Battery)

    Silver Strip 靠近Pawn Shop的维修室
    >Laser Sword (Gems + Flashlight)
    --在这附近获取Machete

    Silver Strip 靠近Humvie (Shamrock)的维修室
    >Heliblade (Toy Helicopter + Machete) -Machete刚才已获得，舞台背后获取Toy Helicopter

    Yucatan Casino 维修室
    >Fire Spitter (Toy Spitball Gun + Tiki Torch)
    >Blazing Aces (Tennis Racket + Tiki Torch) -Tennis Racket在餐厅左边的岩石上
    --从收营台后面的房间里获取LMG、Merc Assault Rifle

    Palisades Mall 2楼靠近商店"Brand New U"的维修室
    >Super Slicer (Serv-bot Mask + Lawnmower)
    >Freedom Bear (Robot Bear + L.M.G.) -从Yucatan获取的LMG, Bear在"Brand New U"店里
    --从2楼商店"High Noon Shooting Range"获取Shotgun

    Palisades 1楼靠近Silver Strip出口过道的维修室
    >Hacker (Flashlight + Computer Case)
    >Flaming Gloves (Boxing Gloves + Motor Oil)
    --从这里的出口到Silver Strip（Hot Excitorama后门）

    Slot Ranch 后台的维修室
    >Gem Blower (Gems + Leaf Blower)
    >Infernal Arms (Training Sword + Motor Oil)

    Food Court 维修室
    >Burning Skull (Bull Skull + Motor Oil) - 商店"Wild West Grill"前面
    >Molotov (Whiskey + Newspaper)
    >Boomstick (Pitchfork + Shotgun) -Shotgun 从 Palisades Mall获取，Pitchfork 在"Wild West Grill"店里
    >Holy Arms (Training Sword + Box of Nails) - Nails在Royal Flush获取, Sword 在"Hamburger Fiefdom"店里
    >WingMan Nectar ("Rojo Diablo"店内，用"橙汁+橙汁"或者"Beer+Jelly Beans"组合 ) + Queen (蜂后杀僵尸或者喝Nectar获得)

    Royal Flush "Tunemakers"店内的维修室
    >Electric Chair (Wheelchair + Battery)
    >Blitzkrieg (Merc Assault Rifle + Electric Chair) - Merc Assault Rifle从Yucatan Casino获取，Electric Chair就是刚才制造的
