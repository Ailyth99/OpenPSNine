    Short career mode


    List of events from the short career mode (played with the latest versions on PS5 so should work on the latest version 1.8.6.1 otherwise will make a note in red):

    The first test at Monza: no medal required, your result will just determine the difficulty but choose the easy difficulty obviously
    Second and third tests at Monza: you just have to get the fastest lap in wet condition and you have to finish under 1:58:00 in night condition to get the gold medal
    Week-end Sprint Race Zolder: you just have to get the fastest lap for each session and finish in the first position for the two races (20min each)
    Brands Hatch Performance Test: you have to beat 7 laps under 11:10:000 on easy difficulty to get the gold medal. Here is my lap time for each
    1:33:715
    1:34:080
    1:33:940
    1:33:710
    1:33:692
    1:34:195
    1:34:826
    Total time: 11:08:158
    Week-end Sprint Race Brands Hatch: you just have to get the fastest lap for each session and finish in the first position for the two races (20min each)
    Fifth test at Misano: you have to do a lap time under 1:46:000 to get the gold medal. I did it with a time of 1:45:937
    Week-end Sprint Race Misano: you just have to get the fastest lap for each session and finish in the first position for the two races (20min each)
    Week-end Sprint Race Hungaroring: you just have to get the fastest lap for each session and finish in the first position for the two races (20min each)
    Sixth test at Nurburgring: you have to do a lap time under 2:03:000 to get the gold medal. I did it with a time of 2:02:677
    Week-end Sprint Race Nurburgring GP: you just have to get the fastest lap for each session and finish in the first position for the two races (20min each)
    Seasonal endurance tests - Paul Ricard: you have to do a lap time under 2:03:000 to get the gold medal. I did it with a time of 2:02:392
    3 hours endurance race weekend at Monza: you just have to get the fastest lap for each session (5min three times) and finish in the top 3 for the race (1 hour). Work only on the disc version which is 1.7.6.1 and play offline in case. Currently not working on the latest version 1.8.11.1
    3 hours endurance race weekend at Silverstone: you just have to get the fastest lap for each session (5min three times) and finish in the top 3 for the race (1 hour). Only did it with the disc version 1.7.6.1 and online so don't know if it works with the latest version.
    Eighth test at Silverstone: not done yet. Ok I did it finally in 2:04:492 and I used dry tires instead of wet and the setup from this very useful video of Blu3_Angels. Got the gold medal with the latest version 1.8.11.1
    Third endurance race at Paul Ricard: not done yet. Again, doesn't work with the latest version because I got none medal after two tries! So I could get the gold medal only with the disc version again! But it was really glitchy because my car was impossible to drive when I had to break and had a hard time to block the AI opponents to make them go to the pit stop, especially the leader. So I finished the race on the second place, but it's enough to get the gold medal because remember, the requirement is just to finish on the top 3.
    Ninth and last test at Spa-Francorchamps: not done yet. I finally did it after more than 5 hours of trying! It's definitely not worth it to choose the Porsche GT3 for the second half of the career mode because of this event. Like Destructor_8 advised, it's better to choose the Bentley GT. Plus, I remember that I got the trophy related to SPA with this car in less than one hour. Check my Youtube video about this lap
    Fourth endurance race at Spa-Francorchamps: Only did it with the disc version 1.7.6.1 and online so don't know if it works with the latest version.
    Fifth and last endurance race at Barcelona: Only did it with the disc version 1.7.6.1 and online so don't know if it works with the latest version.


    Be aware that the lap time required for the test events that I indicated but there are not sure 100%. The lap time I did each time give me the gold medal so it will help you to have an idea of what lap time you should do so try to do like me.



    Your feedback about it will be very much appreciated so don't hesitate to post here and I will update my post to be able to have the most complete career mode guide of ACC on the console version.
