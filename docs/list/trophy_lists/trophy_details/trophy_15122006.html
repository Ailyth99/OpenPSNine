拿戒指的人

从空间站取回伯塔的婚戒。

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • shizhengzhi
    This can be done in the engineering section, inside the mechanical engineering area. Head to the dronebay and unlock the key code door. The password is 0809. Continue down the path and look up to find a ring on the grate above you. Interact to pick it up and then play through the rest of the game. The achievement will unlock at the end of your playthrough. If you have completed the game, you can reload your game and backtrack from the end to any part of the Tacoma.
