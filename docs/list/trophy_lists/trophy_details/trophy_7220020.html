Supply camps are small camps and it's easy to plunder them. The objective is to infiltrate into the camp and steal the chest. You can recognize them by the icon of a carriage. The big "supply camps" counts towards the trophy. The "big camps" are those which needs a key. You need to kill the Keyholder and then you can steal the camp. (This one will give more materials). The supply camps regenerate by themselves, although, the "small camps" regenerate quicker than the big ones.
