    并不是每一种职业外装，是收集每一种人格（刺客，女仆，贵族）身份下特定的收藏物：刺客硬币、巫毒玩偶、宝石。

    There are three different personas in Liberation. Each one has unique collectibles that you can only obtain while wearing the persona in either New Orleans or The Bayou. The three different personas are: the Assassin, the Lady, and the Slave. The collectibles for the Assassin are Assassin Coins, the Lady collects Jeweled Brooches, and the Slave collects Voodoo Dolls. ——from HaoleDave
