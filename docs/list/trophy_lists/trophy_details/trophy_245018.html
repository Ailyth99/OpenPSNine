Pure Bladestone: This one is the worst. There are only two possible locations in the game that you can get this drop. One is the Black Phantom Dual Wielding Black Skeleton that appears after the two Black Phantom Gold Skeletons near the end of level 4-2. It is not recommended to grind this one, as it takes some time to get there and you may also accidentally drop him off the cliff losing its drop. The second and recommended location is again in 4-2 and is dropped by the Black Skeleton in the hidden passage near the beginning of the level. From Adjudicator's Archstone keep moving until you reach the area with the first Reaper. Ignore every enemy and fall down to the left wall as you enter the room. Go through the door, climb the first set of stairs and attack the illusion wall to reveal a hidden passage. At the end of this tunnel is the Black Skeleton. Lock on to him and kill him. If his drop is not a pure, fall off the cliff and retry. As said, it's entirely up to luck, personally it took me 6 hours of grinding to get this one. You can also go online with someone on the same server and level as you and ask him to give you a Pure.
