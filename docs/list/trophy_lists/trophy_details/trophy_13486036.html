A Clean Bill of Health

Have a single character affected by all status ailments (both beneficial and detrimental).

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • whatthetruth
    Heat: Steiner's Flame Saber with Add Status
    Mini: Eiko's or Dagger's WM
    Slow: Vivi's BM
    Stop: Vivi's BM
    Sleep: Vivi's BM
    Virus: Yan at Vile Island (the one with the great pyramid)(Virus Powder)
    Venom: Whale Zombie enemy at almost any beach on disc 3/4 (Venom Breath)
    Freeze: Steiner's Ice Brand with Add Status
    Poison: Vivi's BM (Bio)
    Zombie: Whale Zombie enemy at almost any beach on disc 3/4 (Zombie Powder)
    Berserk: Dagger's WM
    Confuse: Dagger's WM
    Petrify: Vivi's BM
    Silence: Eiko's or Dagger's WM
    Trouble: Zidane's Annoy skill
    Darkness: Dagger's WM
    Doom: Ash enemy in Memoria - Familiar Past (Doom)
    Gradual Petrify: Basilisk in Burmecia (Gradual Petrify)
    Haste: Eiko's WM
    Regen: Eiko's WM
    Float: Eiko's or Dagger WM
    Shell: Eiko's or Dagger's WM
    Vanish: Quina's BLM (Learned from Hornet on Gizamaluke's Grotto)
    Protect: Eiko's or Dagger's WM
    Reflect: Eiko's or Dagger's WM
    Auto-Life: Quina's BLM (Learned from Gimme Cat on Palmnell Island, next to Chocobo Lagoon)

    需要注意的是，Regen和Auto-Life虽然可以靠装备获得自动魔法，但这是不纳入条件的，必须是学习后主动释放，比如Auto-Life，可以通过青魔法来生效。
    还有请最好在Disc4前完成，因为有的附带状态的装备没法购买了，大部分城市被封锁，Disc4里要完成的话异常困难。
