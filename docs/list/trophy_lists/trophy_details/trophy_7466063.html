    There are fifteen different certification tags, each tracking a different statistic.

    Levels
    1.Tag level titles and level-up requirements vary depending on the certification type.
    2.There are ten levels for each tag. The level names are the same on levels 1-5. On levels 6-10, the level names are not consistent between tags, with levels 9 and 10 having a unique level name for each tag.
