    美版对照中文版攻略时玩可能用得到的一些对照：

    三章安洁莉卡的校园委托「Born to be Orbal」选择：Release the clutch slowly/steadily、 Pull in quickly, release slowly、 Strongly at front, lightly at back

    五章技术栋的校园委托「Side by Sidecar」选择：Turn the handlebar right、Slightly to the left、Brake earlier than usual

    六章敏特的校园委托「From Heart to Stomach 」建议料理：Jewel Potage（咖啡店买）、Celestial Pasta Soup（Refreshing Pasta Soup做得）、Wobbly Pudding（Custard Pudding做得）
