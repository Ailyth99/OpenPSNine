明智之举

在生涯模式下的2个特殊赛段之间交叉安装轮胎

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • sawashiro0602
    During your first career rally (Croatia if starting in the Junior WRC), you'll have the opportunity to select your tyres. Make sure you take a mixture of Soft and Hard!

    Now, after the first two stages you'll be able to repair your car.

    Go into the Tyre Selection menu from the left, second from the top, and make sure you set the tyres up so you have, from front left to rear right:

    Hard | Soft
    Soft | Hard

    Spares shouldn't matter.

    You don't have to drive on them! The achievement unlocks when you perform the swap. So you can race the next stage on whatever you like

    外网攻略，就是生涯的青年联赛，一路休息和不参加其余活动到第一场拉力赛，好像是克罗地亚的图，选的最短赛车的话就第一天俩个分段，然后修车，再一个特别分段结束，在第一天俩分段弃权不跑后，下一个分段先不开始，先修车，修车调整轮胎，采用前:棕色胎/绿色胎 后绿色胎/棕色胎，这样交叉对应的类型，换好不用跑下一分段直接跳杯
