    复制poerpyx的攻略：
    Step 1: at the end of Chapter 1, enter the wife’s bedroom upstairs and repair the vanity’s mirror by collecrting the four broken pieces in the room.
    Step 2: at the end of Chapter 3, enter the child’s room upstairs and collect the four bowls scattered across the room. Place the bowls in front of the teddy bears on the cabinet by the bed.
    Step 3: at the end of Chapter 5, head into the bathroom on the bottom floor and collect the three perfume bottles. Then, place them on the tray by the door.
    Step 4: at the end of Chapter 6, collect the 4 paintings found across the house and hang them on the wall above the staircase. The one below the staircase must go where it says Pathetic, the one by the double doors must go where it says Disgusting, the one inside the storeroom must go where it says You’re a Failure, and the one by the piano must go where the red drawing is.
    Step 5: open the double doors and leave the house. Trophy unlocks!
    Step 6: immediately after the trophy unlocks, quit out to the menu and press Continue. This will put you back inside the house, allowing you to continue and eventually finish the painting for :silver: Finished it.
