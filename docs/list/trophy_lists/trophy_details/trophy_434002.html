    搬运一下外站攻略

    Sakura - Successfully complete Arcade mode with Ryu.
    Dan - Successfully complete Arcade mode with Sakura.
    Cammy - Successfully complete Arcade mode with Crimson Viper.
    Gen - Successfully complete Arcade mode with Chun-Li.
    Fei Long - Successfully complete Arcade mode with Abel.
    Rose - Successfully complete Arcade mode with M. Bison.

    Akuma - After unlocking all the characters except for Akuma, Gouken, and Seth, play Arcade mode with any character and set the rounds to 1. Get at least one 'Perfect' victory, and do not lose a single round to fight Akuma after beating Seth. Defeat Akuma to unlock him.

    Gouken - Successfully complete Arcade mode with Akuma, then play Arcade mode with any character and set the rounds to 1. Get at least one 'Perfect' victory, three 'Ultra' combo finishes, five 'First Attack' hits, and do not lose a single round to fight Gouken after beating Seth. Defeat Gouken to unlock him.

    Seth - Successfully complete Arcade mode with all characters, including the bonus characters.
