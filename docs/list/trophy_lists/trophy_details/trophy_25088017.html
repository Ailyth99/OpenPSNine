    油管（watch?v=OGyGsuaxJ9U）上面看到的办法，照着做一次搞定：

    1. Use two controllers, 1P commits fouls within 30m. [DON'T TACKLE WITH YOUR DESIGNATED BLOCKER to avoid being sent off]
    2. 1P moves one defender near the wall; 2P lines up the ball with that defender [BETTER TO BE THE SAME DEFENDER, not mentioned in trophy description, but no harm]
    3. 2P pulls down L3 with minimal force to shoot, resulting in a weak ground ball
    4. Right after shooting, 1P smashes X (PS5, i.e. the pass / chase button) to block
    5. If it is successful, the defender will crouch a bit and cross the leg.
    6. Completed even before half-time of a 5-min game!

    1、使用两名控制员，1P在30米内犯规。[不要与你指定的阻拦者对抗，以免被罚下]

    2、1P将一名防守队员移到墙附近；2P将球与该后卫对齐[最好是同一个后卫，奖杯描述中没有提到，但没有伤害]

    3.2P以最小的射力拉下L3，导致一个弱接地球

    4、射门后，1P击碎X（PS5，即传球/追逐按钮）进行拦截

    如果成功的话，防守队员会稍微蹲下并交叉腿。
