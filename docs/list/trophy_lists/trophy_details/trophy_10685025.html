    II. About Prestige Point Stickers:Q: What are Prestige Point Stickers?
    A: They are little black and white stickers spread throughout the Dead Rising game. If you take a picture of one, you will recieve Prestige Points.

    Q: How will I know if I'll get one in my picture?
    A: You will see a little percentage number appear below the photo box. The closer your number is to 100%, the more points you get.

    Q: What's the highest amount of PP I can get for each sticker?
    A: Without the help of books, each sticker is worth a maximum of 1,000 PP.

    Q: Can I take the same picture of a PP sticker multiple times?
    A: No, you will only get PP from it once.
