Her Last Dance

Defeat the Countess

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • felixus0911


    1 Staring point 起始点– The place where you start the exploration of the dungeon.

    2 Yellow key 黄钥匙– The location of a yellow key, required to open a yellow gate.

    3 Yellow gate 黄门– The location of the yellow gate - you need a yellow key to open it.

    4 Red key 红钥匙(图中标识有误，应该为两格往上的绿色宝箱位置)– The location of the red key. It's required to get inside the room with the boss.

    5 First red gate 红门(5、6任选一)– One of the two red gates leading to the boss room. There's no difference between them - both lead to the same room.

    6 Second red gate 红门(5、6任选一)– One of the two red gates leading to the boss room. There's no difference between them - both lead to the same room.

    7 Boss (Countess) and green key BOSS伯爵夫人和箱子里的绿色钥匙– A room in which the boss of the dungeon, the Countess, awaits you. Once you take down the boss, you will be able to collect a green key from the chest.

    8 Green gate 绿门– The location of the green gate - you need a green key to open it. The key can be acquired after the boss fight with the Countess. The gate needs to be opened to gain access to an optional treasure.

    9 Blue key 蓝钥匙– The location of the blue key.

    10 Mini-boss 中BOSS鳄鱼(可选)– A room with the optional mini-boss, Crocodilian.

    11 Blue gate 蓝门– The location of the blue gate - you need a blue key to open it. It blocks passage to rooms with optional treasure.
