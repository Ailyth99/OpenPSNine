Buddy Points Expert

Reach 10,000 Buddy Points

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

耗时比较长的奖杯，游戏中完成很多活动都会增加亲密点数（与狗狗玩游戏、让狗狗挖道具、开机关等等）。
获得点数累计1W点即可，在狗狗挖掘技能升到3级之后挖一次（同时捡1个金币）可以获得20点，在完成全收集品的过程中基本可以完成70%-80%。

By playing through the story in the game normally, you will likely finish the game on anything between 6000-8500 buddy points (BD). To finish up the remainder, you can achieve a good few 1000's of BD by completing the dog tricks in the later pages of the skills books. They start from level 1 to level 10 with each one rewarding greater buddy points when successfully completed.

You will also amass extra BD when hunting down the remainder of the collectibles, as well as the sniff 'n' dig spots.
