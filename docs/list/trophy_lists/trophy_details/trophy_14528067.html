    EASY method to obtain Trick Weapon Collector trophy


    Install the DLC, and continue your save file
    Go straight to the Golem dungeon, and rush through the 1st floor looking for the death pit with green sparkles (it is guaranteed that one room will have this)
    Drop into the pit and enter the green portal
    Move through the inter-dimensional pathway, and defeat the mini-boss at the end
    Claim your trick weapon from the green chest that appears
    Exit the portal and you will arrive on the 2nd floor of the Golem Dungeon
    Rush through the 2nd floor until you find the green sparkling pit once again
    Enter the portal, kill the mini-boss, loot another weapon from the chest
    Exit the portal and you will arrive on the 3rd floor of the Golem Dungeon
    Use your pendant to quickly exit the dungeon
    Enter the Golem Dungeon again and repeat the previous steps over again until you’ve received all 10 weapons
