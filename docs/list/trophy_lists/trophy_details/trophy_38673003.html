Living On the Field

Play and finish 24 games of at least 3 innings within any mode. (Excludes moments, simulated gameplay, joining game moments in progress.)

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • sadtree1984-we
    这个杯可以快速刷，大概一分钟一场。
    首先在主页右上角设置Gameplay R2切到Misc之后改成:
    CPU pitch delay Faster
    Player lock game flow Skip to next appearance
    Player lock baserunning opportunities none
    Player lock fielding opportunities none
    提交保存后，选经典赛事。presentation mode 改成 Fast Play，innings 3。之后选投手，打手选完按锁定一个人。打完一场不要退出去，直接选confirm restart重复即可。
