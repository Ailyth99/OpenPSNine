  • aliveinsnow
    Easy way to do it: on phu bai valley, just get in a tank and drive up until u can see either point A (American), or the houses surrounding point B (vietnamese). Once at a good vantage point, switch to the gunner and begin marking and shooting ur targets. On operation hastings, the easiest way to do it is to get a tank up on the middle bridge and once again become the gunner. Having an engineer with you helps because ur a lot more exposed than in phu bai. I hate using tanks, and yet I got this in less than 6 games of trying after not trying at all previously.
