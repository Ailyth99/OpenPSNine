奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • ltffly
    Peacock的角色限定杯，使用必杀技Shadow of Impending Doom扔出过所有东西。这招按住任意P不放可以蓄力，不蓄力是LV1，蓄力到阴影有个感叹号闪现是LV2，蓄力到最大是LV3，每个等级扔的东西不同，并且砸中对手才算数。由于完全随机，看RP了，我大概20分钟刷完。

    以下物品列表，看不懂没关系，只要知道一共几种并且你见到了几种就行。

    Level 1 Item Drops:
    •Potted plant
    •Sand bag
    •Old boot
    •Tea cup (with a face)
    •Fish
    •Bowling ball
    •"Jippo" bottle

    Level 2 Item Drops:
    •Loaded safe
    •Tommy 10-Tons
    •Andy Anvil
    •TV with girl from The Ring as played by Filia
    •TV with a Japanese emoticon face
    •TV with Avery
    •Ball and chain
    •Oil drum with a chicken inside (Final Fight)
    •Ms. Fortune head (with Japanese characters saying "Take it Easy!")
    •Kitchen Sink

    Level 3 Item Drops:
    •Feta Fridge (Requiem for a Dream)
    •Elephant
    •Grand piano
    •Easter Island head (Gradius)
    •Hsien-Ko's Tenrai Ha (Darkstalkers)
    •Dio Brando's Road Roller Da! (JoJo's Bizarre Adventure)

    可见这招把各种ACG玩个遍，连东方都有，Level 2的 Kitchen Sink 和 the Level 3 的Dio Brando's Road Roller Da.是稀有掉落。
