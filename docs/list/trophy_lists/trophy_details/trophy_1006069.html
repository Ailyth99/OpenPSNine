Ride of the Valkyrie

Get 50 kills with the Huey

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • aliveinsnow
    1. Join a conquest game on Phu Bai Valley
    2. As soon as the game begins, head for point B and capture the point (once B has been captured, a Huey will immediately spawn)
    3. Head straight for the Huey and get in as the pilot
    4. Turn the Huey 90 degrees to the right (or left) so that one of the side guns is pointing towards the flag
    5. Swap seats so you are the gunner that is facing the flag
    6. Sit and wait for people to turn up!

  • xiaobao382
    1. 加入普白谷的征服游戏
    2. 游戏一开始，直奔B点并占领B点(一旦B点被占领，休伊就会立即生成)
    3.直接飞向休伊号，做飞行员
    4. 将休伊向右(或向左)转动90度，使其中一个侧炮指向国旗
    5. 交换座位，这样你就是面朝国旗的炮手
    6. 坐着等人来吧!
