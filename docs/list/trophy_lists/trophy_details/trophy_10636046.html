Full Deck

Collect all combo cards

奖杯心得 (TIPS)

  • lincoln_bws
    游戏内总共有50张组合卡，其中：

    等级卡（34）: 升满50级，会获得34张组合卡。

    精神病人卡（2）    :
    Flamethrower- Defeat Slappy during the Case “Everyone Knows Slappy” which starts from around 12pm on Day 2 in the Palisades Mall.
    Rocket Launcher- Defeat Roger & Reed during the Case “World's Most Dangerous Trick” which starts from around 12am on Day 4 in the Atlantica Casino.

    生还者卡（4）    :
    Plate Launcher– Go to KokoNutz Sports Town in the Palisades Mall soon after 7am on Day 3 and you will find the door in the back labelled Tape It Or Die unlocked. Speak to the four survivors in here and Wallace will ask you to bring him a Cement Saw and Plates. Do so and he will hand over the Combo Card.
    Blazing Aces– Firstly you must have spoken to the Tape It Or Die guys on Day 3. You must also have completed all
    Case Files up to and including 5-2 but may want to hold off doing 6-1 until after getting the call for the call for the TIOD guys. At 9:15am on Day 4 you will receive a call giving you a Case to rescue them. Bring them back to the Safe House and you will receive the Combo Card.
    Exsanguinator– Firstly you must have spoken to the Tape It Or Die guys on Day 3. You must also have completed all Case Files up to and including 5-2 but may want to hold off doing 6-1 until after getting the call for the call for the TIOD guys. At 9:15am on Day 4 you will receive a call giving you a Case to rescue them. Bring them back to the Safe House and you will receive the Combo Card.
    Power Guitar– Bring Floyd from the Case “Rock Heroes” back to the Safe House and he will give you the Combo Card. “Rock Heroes” starts around 4am on Day 2 on the Silver Strip.

    隐藏卡（6）:
    Tenderizers- Examine the "Blood Round 4" movie poster in the Royal Flush Plaza near the entrance to the Americana Casino.
    Burning Skull- You need to give a total of $1,208,500 to the Fortune Whisperer machine at the entrance to One Little Duck Bingo on the Silver Strip.
    Laser Sword - Examine the "Laser Knight" movie poster at the back of Paradise Platinum Screens on the Platinum Strip.
    Blambow- Examine the "Revenginator 7" movie poster on the side of Cucina Donnacci Italian Restaurant in the Food Court.
    Holy Arms- Examine the "Pit Viking" movie poster on the wall in the South West corner of the Atlantica Casino.
    Freedom Bear- Examine the movie poster right outside the bathroom save point in the Yucatan Casino.
    Paddlesaw - Examine the "Paddle Party Massacre" movie poster in the middle of the Silver Strip just outside the entrance to the Slot Ranch Casino.
    Snowball Cannon- Examine the "Brainfreeze" movie poster just outside the maintenance room on the 2nd floor of the Palisades Mall.

    其他杂项（2）:
    Spiked Bat– On the way back to the Royal Flush Plaza after getting the Maintenance Room Key a short cutscene will play and you will get the Combo Card.
    Wingman– Combine Nectar and a Queen in a maintenance room.
