    這個獎盃名一語三關，Win One for the Gipper是俚語，意指追求成功或勝利以紀念某人（To pursue success or victory in honor of someone else.)。典故出自Notre Dame大學美式足球運動員George Gipp（"the Gipper"）。當時執教Notre Dame隊的教練Knute Rockne呼籲隊員為紀念已經逝世幾年的George Gipp而"Win One for the Gipper"。

    同時Gipper也是美國總統里根的綽號。里根從政前，作爲演員的第一個重要角色是在1940年的經典電影《Knute Rockne, All American》中飾演不幸的美式足球明星George Gipp。片中的名場景之一，奄奄一息，臥床不起的Gipp對他的教練說希望隊友能為他贏得一場關鍵的足球比賽。扮演Gipper的里根在影片中說：“win just one for the Gipper.”這個角色使里根聲名鵲起，從此Gipper成了里根的綽號。

    電影名場景：https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_e7rmpjBSR8

Some time, Rock, when the team is up against it, when things are wrong and the breaks are beating the boys, ask them to go in there with all they've got andwin just one for the Gipper. I don't know where I'll be then, Rock. But I'll know about it, and I'll be happy.
