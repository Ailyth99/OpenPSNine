Daylight Trophies

  • Historian 1 Tips

    Collect every remnant in a playthrough.
    在一个周目里收集所有的Remnant。
    12.50% 非常珍贵

  • Collector 1 Tips

    Collect the thirteen cursed photos.
    收集13幅被诅咒的照片。
    0.90% 极为珍贵

  • Minimalist 1 Tips

    Collect the minimum number of remnants in a playthrough.
    在一个周目中收集最低限度数量的Remnant。
    1.10% 极为珍贵

  • Lone Wolf 1 Tips

    Finish the game using only a single flare.
    仅用一枚Flare通关游戏。
    1.40% 极为珍贵

  • No Stone Unturned 1 Tips

    Collect every single remnant.
    收集每一个Remnant。
    0.80% 极为珍贵

  • Thick Skinned 1 Tips

    Survive 50 witch attacks.
    活过50次女巫的攻击。
    3.40% 极为珍贵

  • A Bright Idea

    Use a flare to keep a witch away.
    使用Flare逼退一个女巫。
    53.60% 一般

  • Sadomasochist 1 Tips

    Finish the game on Hard difficulty.
    以困难难度结束游戏。
    1.70% 极为珍贵

  • Visionary 1 Tips

    Use a glowstick to unveil a secret.
    用荧光棒揭晓一个秘密。
    16.60% 珍贵

  • Just Like Your Mother...

    Confront Dr. Mercer and learn the truth.
    面见默瑟医生并得知真相。
    21.60% 珍贵

  • Outpatient Procedure

    Escape the hospital.
    逃出医院。
    49.40% 珍贵

  • Prison Break

    Escape the prison.
    逃出监狱。
    28.80% 珍贵

  • Breath of Fresh Air

    Escape the sewers.
    逃出下水道。
    24.40% 珍贵

  • Out of the Woods

    Escape the forest.
    逃出森林。
    22.70% 珍贵

  • Full Circle

    Finish the game on any difficulty.
    以任何难度结束游戏。
    21.50% 珍贵

  • Picture of Health 1 Tips

    Finish the game without dying.
    一次不死通关游戏。
    11.50% 非常珍贵
