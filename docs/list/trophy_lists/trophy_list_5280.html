Finish with less than 10 seconds remaining before the level fails
1.Where: Easy 4 – Stop Start
2.Wait until the 1st Lemming falls to the drag-able bar and turn it into a Blocker at the right side of the bar.
3.While the 2nd Lemming is still in the air and is falling to the drag-able, repeatedly tap the Blocker skill so that it immediately blocks upon landing.
4.All the rest of the Lemmings will fall onto the drag-able bar between the 2 Blockers.
5.Once they are all out, drag the bar all the way over to the right.
6.Now speed up the level using the Right button.
7.You have to run the time out for all star ratings.
8.As soon as the top left of the screen shows 1 star and that timer countdown reaches 20 seconds, set the right Blocker to Explode.
