Hawken

  • Overkill

    Eliminate 10 enemies without dying.
    不死的情况下杀死10名敌人。
    18.10% 珍贵

  • Off Guard 1 Tips

    Eliminate an enemy mech that is currently zoomed in.
    消灭一台正在放大瞄准的敌方机甲。
    2.00% 极为珍贵

  • Crushed

    Eliminate an enemy mech by landing on top of it, crushing its robot soul.
    用落在其头上的方法消灭一台敌方机甲，粉碎它的机械之魂。
    1.50% 极为珍贵

  • Terminator

    Eliminate your first mech.
    首次消灭一台机甲。
    63.30% 一般

  • Shut Down

    Eliminate an enemy mech while its weapons are overheated.
    当敌方机甲武器过热的时候消灭它。
    28.90% 珍贵

  • EMPowerless 1 Tips

    Eliminate an enemy while it is under the effect of your EMP.
    消灭一名受到你EMP影响下的敌人。
    0.20% 极为珍贵

  • Survivalist

    Survive for 15 seconds with less than 10% armor, and then eliminate an enemy mech.
    在少于10%护甲的情况下生存15秒，然后消灭一台敌方机甲。
    2.80% 极为珍贵

  • Finisher

    Eliminate an enemy mech while it is repairing.
    当敌方机甲正在维修时消灭它。
    10.90% 非常珍贵

  • I've Made a Huge Mistake

    Deal over 10,000 damage to yourself with your own explosive weapons.
    用你自己的爆炸性武器，对自己的机甲造成10000点以上的伤害。
    1.30% 极为珍贵

  • Eye to Eye

    Eliminate an enemy mech that is zoomed in while you are also zoomed in.
    放大瞄准的时候杀死一名同样也在放大瞄准的敌人。
    0.10% 极为珍贵

  • Basic Training Certification

    Complete the final stage of VR Training and receive your Basic Training Certification.
    完成虚拟现实训练的最终阶段，获得你的基础训练合格证。
    7.30% 非常珍贵

  • Out'a Gas 1 Tips

    Destroy your own mech via fall damage.
    用坠落伤害摧毁你自己的机甲。
    0.60% 极为珍贵

  • Triple Kill

    Get a Triple Kill by eliminating 3 enemies within 10 seconds of each kill.
    通过在十秒以内杀敌三人拿到三杀。
    20.40% 珍贵
