Putty Squad

  • HERO 1 Tips

    Complete all Trophies and defeat Scatterflash to be crowned King Putty
    得到所有奖杯，打败了Scatterflash并加冕为Putty之王
    63.90% 一般

  • TOAD TICKLER

    Collect an 'N' item; then use a Nitro bomb, either as Putty, or from a POD, to blow up a Toad enemy.
    获得一个N道具；然后使用油灰或硝化甘油炸弹炸飞一个蛤蟆敌人
    76.10% 一般

  • ROCKET RIPPER

    Absorb a Firework Imp and then use it as an item in order to kill an enemy with a Firework Imp rocket.
    吸收一个火箭小妖，并将之作为道具消灭一个带着烟火小妖火箭的敌人
    80.40% 一般

  • PUTTY POWER!

    Collect Stars in order to upgrade Putty's attack power. Obtain and use all of his power-ups.
    收集星星以提升油灰攻击力。获取并使用所有增强能量用品
    77.30% 一般

  • NIFTY FIFTY

    Get your on-screen Star Counter up to 50 by collecting the Stars hidden around each level.
    在每一层收集隐藏的星星，将你屏幕上的星星计数器累计到50个
    84.70% 一般

  • STAR CATCHER

    Get your on-screen Star Counter up to 200 by collecting the Stars hidden around each level.

  • JELLY BELLY

    Knock out Dweezil the cat; then use his tummy to bounce to a higher area.
