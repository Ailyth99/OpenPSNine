Paparazzi Trophies

  • Quite dashing! 1 Tips

    Dash 200 times in one level.
    在一关之内冲刺200次。
    28.50% 珍贵

  • Superdecoys! 1 Tips

    Combine the fire drink and the decoys to get 8 fire-powered decoys.
    将火焰酒和诱饵组合起来得到8个强力火焰诱饵。
    25.30% 珍贵

  • Zing! 1 Tips

    Pass through a single set of trap doors 3 times in one level.
    在一关之内通过3个单组的“陷阱门”。
    24.70% 珍贵

  • Smashing! 1 Tips

    Destroy the Pringo Dingo ice sculpture.
    毁掉Pringo Dingo的冰雕。
    36.00% 珍贵

  • Back-and-Forth! 1 Tips

    Play a match where the lead changes over 10 times.
    在一关中让领先者的位置交替10次。
    38.30% 珍贵

  • Un-a-peel-ing! 1 Tips

    Finish a match without hitting a single banana.
    在不踩到香蕉的情况下完成一场比赛。
    37.70% 珍贵

  • Not so fast! 1 Tips

    Get 5 pictures of the celebrity while they're using the fire drink.
    在明星享用火焰酒时拍下他的5张照片。
    18.20% 珍贵

  • Supersight! 1 Tips

    Get 5 pictures of the celebrity while they're invisible.
    在明星隐形时拍下他的5张照片。
    20.80% 珍贵

  • Always Undercover! 1 Tips

    Spend less than 15 seconds out of cover in one level.
    在一关之内没有掩护的时间少于15秒。
    14.20% 非常珍贵

  • Sharpshooter! 1 Tips

    Successfully hit more pictures than you miss and win the game.
    使你成功拍到的照片多于错失的，并在当前游戏中获胜。
    42.00% 珍贵

  • Cheater! 1 Tips

    Get 5 pictures of the celebrity before the level even starts.
    在一关开始之前就已经拍到5张明星的照片。
    31.70% 珍贵

  • Marathon!

    Play all 6 levels in a row with one character.
    使用一个角色连续完成全部6关游戏。
    18.90% 珍贵
