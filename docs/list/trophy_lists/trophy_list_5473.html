flOw

  • Zen 1 Tips

    Leave game on idle in the end of credits for 5 minutes.
    在制作人列表处闲置游戏五分钟。
    17.90% 珍贵

  • Beauty 1 Tips

    Complete any campaign without being hurt.
    完成任意一关没有收到伤害。
    24.70% 珍贵

  • Peace 1 Tips

    Complete any campaign without eating anything.
    不进食任何生物完成任意一关。
    18.60% 珍贵

  • Swift 1 Tips

    Complete any campaign within 120 seconds.
    以120秒完成任意一关。
    32.80% 珍贵

  • Adventure

    Start the first campaign after installing.
    在安装游戏后开始游玩。
    89.80% 一般

  • Evolve 1 Tips

    Unlock the second creature.
    解锁第二种生物。
    53.00% 一般

  • Giant 1 Tips

    Complete any campaign with a creature of 25 segments or longer.
    生物以25个分支或是以上完成任意一关。
    27.50% 珍贵

  • Fashion 7 Tips

    Decorate your creature with more than 10 alternating segments.
    让你的生物拥有十个以上的分支。
    25.40% 珍贵

  • Vegetarian 2 Tips

    Eat all foods in a campaign without killing any advanced creature.
    进食除高等生物以外的食物完成任意一关。
    16.60% 珍贵

  • Cannibalism 6 Tips

    Eat all advanced creatures in a campaign without eating any food.
    在任意一关中进食所有的高等生物而不进食任何食物。
    15.80% 珍贵

  • Gratitude

    Consume all credit foods.
    进食在制作人列表一关中的所有食物。
    20.80% 珍贵

  • Devious

    Paralyze the same creature 5 times without killing it.
    麻痹同一生物而不把它杀死。
    18.40% 珍贵

  • Gaia

    Eat every level complete egg.
    进食每一关中的结束蛋（点）。
    25.20% 珍贵

第1个DLC

  • Grace 2 Tips

    Consume 10 winged foods.
    进食十个有羽翼的生物。
    18.10% 珍贵
