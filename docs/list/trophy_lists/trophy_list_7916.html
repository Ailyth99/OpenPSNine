穿越空间

  • Overclocked

    Collect your first power-up. You did? Now go and wipe them all out!
    拿到你的首个强化道具并用此消灭敌人
    86.80% 一般

  • Inbox (1)

    Retrieve a dropped message from a fellow ship in the RM fleet. There is one awaiting you just at the end of Level01!
    从RM舰队的友军舰船那里收到一封消息.在第一关结尾就有一艘舰船在等着你！
    59.00% 一般

  • Fragmentation

    Crush 10 boulders in Level04. Feels good, doesn't it...
    Your lasers are your friend here...
    在第四关碾碎10艘博尔德.是不是感觉不错...在这里你的激光是你的朋友...
    15.90% 珍贵

  • Mr. Anderson!

    Get infected by the IPA virus in your first encounter with it in Level05.
    Oh wait, have you updated your anti-virus database? Good for you.
    在第五关第一次遇见IPA病毒的时候被感染.噢等等,你有没有更新过你的反病毒数据库？这对你有益.
    10.10% 非常珍贵

  • Trip The Light Fantastic

    Sustain 10 seconds unharmed and without shooting against the boss IPA_IRecon in Level09.
    On the bright side, you get to enjoy all the lightworks!
    在第九关的时候保持10秒既不攻击IPA_IRecon也没有受到伤害的状态.往好处想的话,你可以有机会欣赏所有这么棒的光影！
    0.90% 极为珍贵

  • Keep Calm and Don't Shoot

    Pass through the low-energy galaxy in Level10 without shooting a single bolt. It... is... so... tempting... yet...
    在第十关以完全不射一发闪电的情况下通过低能量银河.它...是...如此...诱人...但...
    1.00% 极为珍贵

  • Double delete 2 Tips

    The boss Shift_2LR in Level11 comes disguised behind two Mutex_3S shells. Popping one shell is easy, but are you fast enough to pop the other one too?
    在第11关的Shifr_2LR躲在2个Mutex_3S炮弹后面.打掉一个是很简单的,但你可以快到打掉另外一个吗？
    0.70% 极为珍贵

  • Try Catch Finally 2 Tips

    Make it unharmed through all the fleeing enemies in Level19... and prepare for what's coming next...
    在第19关以无伤的状态通过所有在逃走的敌人...准备记下来要发生什么吧...
    0.50% 极为珍贵

  • Red Pill or Blue Pill? Neither 1 Tips

    In Level20, you may go through one of two gates: Red or Blue... Rumors say there exists yet a third green gate... it lies [beyond, beyond, beyond] the two gates.
    Can you discover the third green gate?
    在第20关,你有可能需要穿过红色或者蓝色的门...据流言说这里好像存在第三个绿门...它离另外两扇门很远很远很远....你能发现这第三扇绿门吗？
    0.50% 极为珍贵

  • Garbage Collector

    The final boss encounter in Level29 is a crowded one. Wipe out 30 of the small bacterial minions puffed out by IPA_DRecurse spores.
    在第29关的最终头目是一群一群的.消灭30个被IPA_DRecurse孢子召唤出来的小细胞仆从.
    0.60% 极为珍贵

  • The Bad... 1 Tips

    Defeat IPA_IRecon: the first incarnation of the virus in Level09.
    Now you really know the true enemy!
    打败IPA_IRecon:在第九关的病毒的第一化身.现在你真的知道真正的敌人是谁了！
    1.50% 极为珍贵

  • The Ugly...

    Defeat IPA_VInterface: the second incarnation of the virus in Level19.
    It comes back to you for vengeance. This time it will not leave you without paying a price!
    打败IPA_Vinterface:在19关的病毒的第二化身.它为了复仇而归来.这次你不付出代价它是不会离开的！
    0.70% 极为珍贵

  • ...And The Worst

    Defeat IPA_DRecurse: the third incarnation of the virus in Level29.
    This is the final battle. Only one will survive!
    打败IPA_DRecurse:在第29关病毒的第三化身.这是最终战斗了.只有一个能生存！
    0.60% 极为珍贵

  • #include "*.*" 2 Tips

    Discover and collect all Secret Orbs in Hyper Void... and enjoy the level shortcuts!
    在游戏里发现以及收集所有秘密宝珠...还有享受每个关卡的近道！
    0.40% 极为珍贵

  • To The Last Kilo-byte 2 Tips

    Win all 29 levels in Hyper Mode. Be proud, you have seen each and every kilo-byte this game has to offer.
    以Hyper模式的情况下赢得所有29关.自豪吧,你已经看见这个游戏提供的每一以及所有字节的内容了.
    0.40% 极为珍贵
