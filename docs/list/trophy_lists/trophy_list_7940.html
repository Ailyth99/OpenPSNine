AirMech Arena

  • Getting Started

    Complete the Tutorial.
    完成教学关
    13.50% 非常珍贵

  • On a Roll

    Win 3-consecutive online matches.
    连续赢3局在线对战
    16.40% 珍贵

  • Win 10 Online Matches

    Win 10 or more online matches in any game mode.
    在任意模式中赢10局或以上在线对战
    8.40% 非常珍贵

  • Max Level

    Reach Level 15 in any AirMech in an online or offline game.
    在任意AirMech达到15级，在线或离线都可
    26.20% 珍贵

  • My hands are clean...

    Win a match, online or offline, without destroying any units with your AirMech.
    在不摧毁任何单位的情况下赢得一场在线或离线对战
    7.90% 非常珍贵

  • Gladiator

    Accumulate 100 direct AirMech Kills online against human opponents.
    在线模式累计杀死100次玩家的AirMech
    0.10% 极为珍贵

  • Damage Inc.

    Do 1,000,000 damage with your AirMech.
    用你的AirMech造成1000000伤害
    11.50% 非常珍贵

  • Sole Survivor

    Win a solo survival match on any Survival map.
    在任意生存地图中赢得一场Solo生存对战
    0.40% 极为珍贵

  • The Four Horsemen

    Win a 4-player online survival match on any Survival map.
    在任意生存地图赢得一场4人在线生存对战
    0.90% 极为珍贵

  • Interceptor

    Accumulate 20 human piloted enemy AirMech kills, each made while the enemy is carrying a unit.
    累计击杀20次人类驾驶敌对的AirMech，且击杀时该敌人携带着一个单位
    0.10% 极为珍贵

  • Serious Competitor

    Win 200 matched games.
    赢得200局匹配对战
    0.10% 极为珍贵

  • Challenger: 45 Stars 1 Tips

    Accumulate 45 Stars in the Challenges.
    在挑战中累积获得45颗星
    1.40% 极为珍贵

  • Rock You Like a Hurricane!

    Win an online match where 100% of the enemy's base health is taken within 1 minute.
    在在线模式中1分钟以内使敌方基地血量变为0并赢得胜利。
    18.00% 珍贵

  • Blood Thirsty

    Destroy 3 human piloted AirMechs in the first 3 minutes of an online match.
    在在线对战模式中摧毁三个玩家驾驶的AirMechs在开局三分钟以内
    0.10% 极为珍贵
